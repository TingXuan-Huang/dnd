# Charmed

While you have the Charmed condition:

- **Can't Harm the Charmer.** You can't attack the charmer or target the charmer with damaging abilities or magical effects.
- **Social Advantage.** The charmer has [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on any [[ability-check]] to interact with you socially.

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#CharmedCondition]

## Notable non-effects

Being Charmed does **not** make you act on the charmer's behalf (despite the social Advantage), make you defend the charmer, or expose you to friendly fire restrictions. The charm is a social/protection bias, not full mind control. (For mind control, see *Dominate Person*, which uses different rules.)

## Common sources

- Spells: *Charm Person*, *Friends* cantrip, *Hypnotic Pattern*, etc.
- Various monster abilities (vampire bite, succubus, kelpie)

## Defenses

- [[elf]] **Fey Ancestry** grants [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on saves to avoid being Charmed.
- [[gnome]] **Gnomish Cunning** grants Advantage on [[intelligence|Int]], [[wisdom|Wis]], and [[charisma|Cha]] [[saving-throw|saves]] — most charm saves use Wis.
- **Mindless Rage** (Berserker Barbarian L6 in PHB 2024 — page is a [[path-of-the-berserker|stub]] in this wiki) grants immunity while raging.
