# Module 1 — DM Flow Cheat

**One page. Keep open during play.** Pair with `module-1-play.html` (read-aloud) and `module-1-npcs.html` (voices). Refer to `module-1-checks.md` only when a roll comes up you don't remember.

## The two clocks

```
DAY CLOCK   Sunrise → Forenoon → Midday → Afternoon → Sunset → Night
            [Act I: free explore — 4 slots before bell]    [Act II–IV]

AWAKE CLOCK 0 ─ 1 ─ 2 ─ 3 ─ 4 ─ 5 ─ 6
            asleep   listen  warn  defend  call  WAKE
            Target end of Module 1: stage 1–3. Never above 4.
```

Hide the Awakening Clock. Tick the Day Clock visibly.

## Free vs Fixed — visual map

```
ACT I       SUNRISE
            ▼
            [FIXED] Bent Plow opening · Mordren's offer
            ▼
            [FREE]  4 slots · pick 3–4 of 7 locations
                    1 inn · 2 hall · 3 shrine · 4 smith
                    5 grain · 6 mill · 7 cottage
            ▼
            [FIXED] SUNSET BELL · 3 choices (A/B/C, all → descent)
            ▼
ACT II      [FIXED] Descent stairs · read-aloud
            ▼
            [FIXED] Central hall reveal · 3 tunnels
            ▼
ACT III     [FREE]  Tunnel mouth peek · any order · no combat
                    L blue · M sound · R wind
            ▼
            [FIXED] POOL SHUDDER · exit cue
            ▼
ACT IV      [FIXED] Surface + cliffhanger (one of three endings)
            ▼
            END SESSION
```

**Rule:** Players control direction inside FREE blocks. DM controls transitions between FIXED blocks. Never break this contract — even if it means narrating Mordren saying "Out, now."

## Pacing budget (3.5 hr session)

| Block | Target | Hard cap |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-game prep | 5 min | 10 min |
| Act I opening (inn) | 15 min | 20 min |
| Act I free day (4 slots) | 50 min | 70 min |
| Sunset Bell | 15 min | 20 min |
| Act II descent | 25 min | 35 min |
| Act III sampling | 45 min | 60 min |
| Act IV cliffhanger | 20 min | 25 min |
| Logging & out | 5 min | 10 min |
| **Total** | **3h 00m** | **3h 50m** |

If you're 30 min over by Sunset Bell: ring the bell mid-conversation. Players notice the rail; that's OK — it's a clock, not a railroad.

## What the party should walk away with

Score 2+ of these for a "smooth" Module 1. Below 2, Bran will be skeptical and the village will be tense in Module 2.

- [ ] Bran's permission (or paid bond, or forced past)
- [ ] A blessing, torch, or sharpened weapon
- [ ] The goblin clue (grain store OR sound tunnel)
- [ ] Garret's blue-light testimony
- [ ] A guess at "House of First Light"
- [ ] At least one mouth peeked into all three tunnels
- [ ] The broken dagger taken (or recognized in place)

## Awakening Clock — quick triggers

| Trigger | +N |
|---|---|
| Leveled spell cast (each) | +1 |
| Broke royal seal in cottage | +1 |
| Refused descent (option C) | +1 |
| Each tunnel mouth peeked | +1 |
| Touched a Blue mote | +1 |
| Shouted in Sound mouth | +1 |
| Spoke own name in Wind mouth | +2 |
| Pushed past a tunnel mouth | +2 |
| 10+ HP spilled in ruin | +1 |

**Stage feedback to narrate:**

- 1 — dust shifts; torch flickers wrong
- 2 — carvings flicker blue in periphery
- 3 — whispered name; one PC Wisdom save DC 11 or Frightened 1 round
- 4 — floors creak before footsteps
- 5 — surface bell rings once without hands
- 6 — END SESSION (pool fills black; no rest in ruin)

## Cliffhanger trigger card

```
ENDING A · MORDREN DISAPPEARS
  Trigger: option B chosen, OR cottage broken into,
           OR rude to him at inn, OR clock ≥ 4

ENDING B · SILVER LEAVES IN WELL
  Trigger: party took anything physical from ruin
           (dagger / nail cord / stone chip)

ENDING C · FORGOTTEN NAME
  Trigger: ignored 1+ NPC, OR skipped 3+ locations,
           OR cast leveled spell in town

If two trigger: pick the one with the more vivid image.
If none trigger: ending A by default (Mordren goes alone).
```

## If players push past a tunnel mouth

Allow **one** room only. No boss. The room ends with environmental retreat (corridor closes, ward flickers, floor groans). Award one extra clue from below, then force them out. Clock +2.

| Tunnel | One-room concession |
|---|---|
| Blue | Glimpse of a half-rusted shortsword and a kneeling armored skeleton — its blue ribs are dark. They will not see Edras tonight. The skeleton does not move. |
| Sound | Three goblin minions playing dice with knucklebones. The goblins scatter the moment they're seen — they don't fight tonight. Find one stolen child's wooden horse (Sister Elra recognizes it). |
| Wind | The trap floor. Anyone running gets DEX DC 12, 1d6 piercing on fail. Walking carefully: auto pass. No archive, no map this session. |

## What you can drop if you're short on time

In rough order of "first to cut":

1. **Mordren's cottage** (skip unless party invites scrutiny). Lose royal-seal clue — picks up in M2 anyway.
2. **Grain store visit** (skip if Sound tunnel mouth gets peeked — same clue).
3. **The third tunnel peek** (if 30 min over by Act III, fire the Pool Shudder after the 2nd).
4. **Persuading Bran in detail** (fall back to "pay the bond, descend").

What you should **never** cut:

- The opening at the inn (it's the social contract).
- The Sunset Bell pivot (it's the discipline of the session).
- The Descent read-aloud (it's the atmosphere check for the whole ruin).
- One full cliffhanger ending (the session has to land).

## If something breaks at the table

| Problem | Move |
|---|---|
| Player wants to skip the village entirely | Bran physically blocks the stairs. He has six villagers with him. Roll a hard Persuasion or pay. |
| Player wants to bypass Mordren | Fine — Mordren goes alone overnight. Bring him back missing in Act IV. |
| Combat erupts in the village | Bessa, Tamsin, and Bran all intervene. Use Commoner stats × 4. End in arrest or expulsion. Module 1 ends early in disgrace. |
| TPK risk somewhere | You shouldn't be running combat in M1. If a trap or fall threatens death — pull the punch silently. Module 1 is a setup, not a meatgrinder. |
| Player tries to leave Fallowton | Mordren's coin doubles. Henric offers his savings. Bran says "If you leave, do not come back." Make staying easier than leaving without removing the choice. |

## After session — DM checklist (5 min)

1. Save the session log above.
2. Update the Player Choice Log (in your campaign journal) — what they did, who they liked.
3. Tick the Awakening Clock stage forward by **0–1 more** if the session ended on a calm note (the ruin keeps thinking). Skip if it's already at 5+.
4. Decide which tunnel will be the focus of Module 2 based on what the party reacted to most. If unclear, let the players decide at the start of next session ("you've slept on it — which one calls to you?").
5. Note any NPC the party ignored or harmed — write one sentence on how that NPC's behavior shifts next session.
