# Passive Perception

The default value the DM uses when a [[perception]] check is required but they don't want to roll — e.g., to notice hidden threats or to spot ambushes.

## Formula

`10 + your Perception modifier` (Wis modifier + [[proficiency-bonus]] if proficient, doubled if you have [[expertise]]). [BR-2024 §01-creating-a-character#Key Numbers]

If you have a general source of [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on Perception checks, add +5 to your passive Perception. With Disadvantage, subtract 5.
