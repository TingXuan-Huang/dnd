# Boon of Spell Recall

> Category: Epic Boon · Prerequisites: Level 19+ (Epic Boon class feature)

- **Ability Bonus:** Increase one ability score by 1, to a maximum of 30. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Epic Boon Feats]
- **Spell Recall:** When you cast a spell using a [[spell-slot]] of level 1–4, roll 1d4. On a roll of 1, the slot is not expended. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Epic Boon Feats]
