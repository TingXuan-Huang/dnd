# Ability Score Improvement

> Category: General · Prerequisites: Level 4+ · **Repeatable**

Increase one ability score of your choice by 2, or two ability scores by 1 each. You cannot raise a score above 20 with this feat. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Ability Score Improvement]
