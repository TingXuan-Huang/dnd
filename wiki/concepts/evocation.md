# Evocation

> School of magic · Typical effects: Energy, damage

Evocation spells channel energy into damage or other effects — fire, lightning, force, light. Basic Rules examples: *Burning Hands*, *Magic Missile*, *Thunderwave*, *Guiding Bolt*. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Schools of Magic]
