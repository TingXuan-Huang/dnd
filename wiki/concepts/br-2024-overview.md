# D&D Beyond Basic Rules (2024) — Overview

The free `br-2024` ruleset is Wizards of the Coast's no-cost digital release for the 2024 D&D revision ("5.5e"). It's a **subset** of the full 2024 Player's Handbook, scoped for zero-cost play. [BR-2024 §00-overview]

## Key changes from 5e (2014)

- **Background-driven origin.** [[character-origins|Background grants ability scores + Origin feat]] rather than racial ASI.
- **Weapon mastery.** [[weapon-mastery|New class feature]] for martial classes.
- **Origin feats at level 1.** Every PC gets one (from their [[character-origins|background]]).
- **No racial ASI.** Species traits are mechanical (Darkvision, etc.) but ability bonuses come from background.

## What's NOT in the free Basic Rules

The Basic Rules omit most subclasses, additional species, additional backgrounds, and many spells. Those live in the full 2024 PHB. When playing with paid content, defer to PHB 2024.

For the **Artificer** class specifically: not in PHB 2024 — it's planned to come from Unearthed Arcana 2024 (we'll source into `raw/artificer/` separately).

## Risk areas

- **2014 vs 2024 mismatch** — see [[2014-vs-2024]].
- **Background ASI stacking** — apply background bonuses last, cap 20 at level 1.
- **Weapon mastery** — requires class feature; track masteries on sheet and swap on [[long-rest]].
- **Multiclass spell slots** — use [[multiclass-spellcasting]] table.
