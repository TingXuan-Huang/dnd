# Module 2 — Interactive & Decisions

Player-facing choices, skill checks, branches, and **Ruin Awakening Clock** triggers. When a **【DM DECISION】** appears, pause and confirm with yourself (or co-DM) before ruling.

## Act 2 — Village debate (interactive)

### Bran's permission conflict

Before the party descends, Elder Bran tries to stop them from treating the sinkhole like an open dungeon. This is a small social obstacle, not a campaign blocker.

**Default challenge:** one PC speaks for the group.

| Approach | Check | Result |
|----------|-------|--------|
| Promise to protect Fallowton and return village goods | Charisma (Persuasion) DC 13 | Bran allows descent without payment |
| Argue that knowledge is safer than ignorance | Charisma (Persuasion) or Intelligence (History/Arcana) DC 14 | Bran allows descent, but demands a report |
| Threaten, mock, or bypass him publicly | Charisma (Intimidation) DC 15 | Success: descent allowed but village trust drops; fail: bond required |
| Offer money first | No check | Pay village bond; descent allowed |
| Sneak in without permission | Dexterity (Stealth) group DC 13 | On fail, village trust drops and Bran may seal entrance later |

**Village bond if they fail or refuse:** 10 GP total for the party, or one useful item left as collateral. The money is framed as compensation for risk to Henric's field and village labor, not an entry fee.

**On a strong success:** Bran sends one villager to bring rope, torches, or a simple warning charm; gain advantage on one social check with Fallowton villagers after returning.

**Bran line:**

> "You want to walk under our fields, disturb our dead earth, and call it courage? Then convince me Fallowton is not the one paying your price."

### NPC goals (quick)

| NPC | Wants | If PCs agree… |
|-----|-------|----------------|
| Bran | Seal the hole | No descent, or seal after one trip |
| Henric | Money / field restored | Share of gold; blame wizard if none |
| Elra | No magic disturbed | Disapproval if PCs flaunt magic items |
| Mordren | Study; relics to him | Interest ↑; may enter ruin alone later |

### Sample player questions → answers

| Player asks… | Bran | Henric | Elra | Mordren |
|--------------|------|--------|------|---------|
| Is it safe? | “No hole under earth is safe.” | “My field wasn’t safe.” | “Holy signs have failed.” | “Safer than ignorance.” |
| Was anyone down before? | “Old stories only.” | “My grandfather spoke of stones…” **【if H.V. dagger】** | “Pray we are not the second sin.” | “Many places; this one is… fresh.” |
| What do we get? | Village gratitude | A share of **my** field’s treasure | Souls unharmed | Knowledge; fair payment for artifacts |

### Decision: Do the PCs descend now?

| Choice | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Descend today** | Clock starts at 0; rain still soft above |
| **Wait until morning** | +1 village scene; goblins may deepen nest; Mordren scouts |
| **Try to seal** | Conflict with Mordren/Henric; Elder support |
| **Only scout stairs** | Short scene; partial carvings; clock +0 unless loud |

## Ruin Awakening Clock (secret tracker)

**Start:** Stage 0. **Do not tell players the number** — describe symptoms from Module 1.

### Advance +1 when any occur (stack same scene)

- Loud noise (arguing, unshielded hammer, explosion spell)  
- Blood spilled (10+ HP total in ruin, or ritual blood)  
- Spell slot spent underground  
- Relic taken without brief respect (touch altar, kick bones) **【DM: strict or lenient】**  
- Trap triggered  
- Undead desecrated before parley attempt  
- Goblin escapes to warn Grask  
- Long rest inside ruin **【DM DECISION: allow rest?】**  
- 30+ minutes real-time arguing in one room  

### Stage effects (mechanical hooks)

| Stage | Mechanical option |
|-------|-------------------|
| 3 Warning | One PC: WIS save DC 11 or **Frightened** until end of next turn (whispered name) |
| 4 Defending | Next trap with **advantage**; or +1 skeleton in Blue Light |
| 5 Calling | Mordren descends OR surface event (bells ring without hands; a villager forgets an old name) |
| 6 Waking | End session; pool floods; no more short rests in ruin |

**Tunnel return endings** (advance clock +0 unless they caused chaos):

- Blue done → vision in pool (black spring / white tree)  
- Goblin boss dead → wet laugh echo (+1 stage optional)  
- Map taken → silence + 1" black water (+1 stage optional)

# Tunnel 1 — Blue Light (playable branch)

## Room 1: Hall of Blue Motes

**Atmosphere:** Motes drift near cracks and old bloodstains.

| Action | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| Touch a mote | **No damage.** WIS save DC 11 or vision (see below) |
| Swat / destroy mote | Clock +1; next room undead hostile |
| Proceed quietly | Advantage on first stealth vs undead |

**Vision (fail or willing touch):**

> Soldiers running through the ruin. Someone shouts, “Seal the lower spring!” A woman carries a child made of light. Then darkness.

**Skill: Religion or Arcana DC 13** — motes are ward-fragments, not souls.

---

## Room 2: Dead Guard Post

**Setup:** Skeletons slumped; weapons rusted but **edges oddly clean**.

| Approach | Encounter |
|----------|-----------|
| **Respectful** (whisper, cover bones, no loot yet) | Undead **slow**: last in initiative; Edras hears intent |
| **Immediate loot** | **Hostile wake** (see Module 3 encounter tiers) |
| **Offer prayer / oath** | Religion DC 12: one skeleton does not rise; clock −0 |

**Parley line (if one animates):**

> “The oath… still burns. Speak quickly.”

---

## Room 3: Edras, Last Guard of the Blue Flame

**First contact:** He steps from blue-lit dark; ribs glow.

### Player options

| Choice | Process | Likely result |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| **Fight** | Normal combat; no quarter unless he drops to 0 and DM mercy | Loot room; clock +1 |
| **Lie** (“We are priests”) | Insight DC 14 (Edras); Deception vs Insight | On fail → fight |
| **Truth: we seal evil / help village** | Persuasion DC 13 (+2 if they were respectful) | Test question below |
| **Show goblin trophy / map** | Context | Advantage if anti-goblin; suspicion if greedy |

**Edras test question:**

> “What do you carry that the dark did not give you?”

**Good answers:** hope, duty to living, village names, refusal of greed.  
**Bad answers:** gold first, “power,” Mordren’s orders without explanation.

**Success:** Edras allows **one** item from reliquary; warns about Black Spring; does not leave room.

**Failure:** Fight or “pass but cursed” — next long rest: dream of blue fire.

### Reward choice **【DM DECISION】**

| Item | When to pick |
|------|----------------|
| **Lantern of First Light** | Default; campaign lore |
| **Star-Iron Dagger** | If dagger clue is central |
| **Oath-Bound Shield** | If party is squishy |

## Room 3: Gold Pit — Grask the Split-Ear

**Terrain:** Cracked offering pit; gold mixed with rot.

| Approach | Grask behavior |
|----------|----------------|
| Charge | Fight; +1 goblin only if warned |
| Negotiate (“share gold”) | Deception DC 14; he betrays when weak |
| Intimidation | DC 15 or he fights dirty (disengage, oil, fire) |

**On death:** Weird sounds stop → **callback** wet laugh from all tunnels.

**Loot:** coins, silver wheat amulet, **black coin** (never warms) — identify Arcana DC 15: wrongness.

## Room 2: Floor That Remembers

**Trap:** Wrong path / run across carved footprints.

| Trigger | Save | Effect |
|---------|------|--------|
| Careless crossing | DEX DC 12 | Fail: 1d6 piercing (stone needles); Success: half |
| Run | Disadvantage on save | |
| Follow carved **guard** prints only | Auto success if noticed (Investigation DC 13) |

**Stage 4+ note:** The floor whispers and shakes, but do not add the ghostly blade sweep in Session 1.

---

## Room 3: Breathless Archive

**Map:** Silver-bark; floats until touched.

| Touch map | Torches out 1 round; then reveals 3 glowing sites |
|-----------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| Refuse | Can return; Edras/Mordren want it later |
| Mordren sees it later | He may bargain or steal **【DM DECISION】** |

**Arcana DC 14:** map reacts to moonlight, blood, spellcasting, ancient words.

**Carved trio (puzzle):** PCs who match “memory saves the world” get advantage on next WIS save in ruin.

**Breathless Warden:** If the map is touched carelessly, the puzzle fails, or the clock is high, the Animated Armor guardian can wake. If the party already fought Dust Mephits in this tunnel, the Warden should be solved through the phrase, silence, or retreat rather than becoming a second full fight.

## When to pause for 【DM DECISION】

Before session, decide: README table rows 1–7.

During session, pause if:

- Player wants to redeem goblins (contradicts evil default)  
- Player romances Mordren backstory you haven’t set  
- TPK risk on Floor That Remembers (lower DC or warning)  
- They want to rest inside ruin (clock +1 vs force surface rest)  

**Principle:** Offer choices; let consequences stand; check with yourself before new NPCs become canon.
