# Wisdom

> Measures: Awareness, willpower. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#The Six Abilities]

## Where Wisdom applies

- **[[ability-check|Wisdom checks]]:** noticing things, reading behavior, intuiting truth.
- **[[saving-throw|Wisdom saves]]:** resisting mental influence (charm, fear, compulsion).
- **Spellcasting ability** for Cleric, Druid, Ranger.
- **Skills:** [[animal-handling]], [[insight]], [[medicine]], [[perception]], [[survival]].
