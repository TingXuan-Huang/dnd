# Sneak Attack

> [[rogue]] feature · Gained at level 1

Once per turn, deal an extra **1d6** damage to one creature you hit with an [[attack-roll]] if:

- You had [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on the attack, **OR**
- An enemy of the target was within 5 ft of it and not [[incapacitated]].

You can't have [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on the attack. The damage die scales with rogue level (per the full Rogue class table). [BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Rogue]

## Weapon restriction

The attack must be made with a [[finesse]] or ranged weapon.
