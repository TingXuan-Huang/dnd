Traits

***Pack Tactics.*** The hobgoblin has Advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the hobgoblin’s allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally doesn’t have the [Incapacitated](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#IncapacitatedCondition) condition.

Actions

***Longsword.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +3, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 12 (2d10 + 1) Slashing damage.

***Longbow.*** *Ranged Attack Roll:* +3, range 150/600 ft. *Hit:* 5 (1d8 + 1) Piercing damage plus 7 (3d4) Poison damage.
