***Running Leap.*** With a 10-foot running start, the tiger can Long Jump up to 25 feet.

***Rend.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +6, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) Slashing damage.

Bonus Actions

***Nimble Escape.*** The tiger takes the [Disengage](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#DisengageAction) or [Hide](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#HideAction) action.
