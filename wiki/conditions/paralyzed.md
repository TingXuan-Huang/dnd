# Paralyzed

While you have the Paralyzed condition:

- **[[incapacitated|Incapacitated]].** You have the Incapacitated condition.
- **Speed 0.** Your [[speed]] is 0 and can't increase.
- **Saving Throws Affected.** You automatically fail [[strength|Str]] and [[dexterity|Dex]] [[saving-throw|saves]].
- **Attacks Affected.** [[attack-roll|Attack rolls]] against you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]].
- **Automatic Critical Hits.** Any attack roll that hits you is a [[critical-hit]] if the attacker is within 5 feet of you.

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#ParalyzedCondition]

## Common sources

- Spells: *Hold Person*, *Hold Monster* (Wis save at end of each turn)
- Monster abilities: ghoul claw, carrion crawler tentacles
