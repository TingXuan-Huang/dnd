# Ability Score Generation

The DM picks one of three methods to set your six [[ability-scores]] at character creation. [BR-2024 §01-creating-a-character#Step 3]

## Method 1 — Standard Array

Assign these six values in any order: **15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8**.

### Suggested assignments by class

| Class | STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Barbarian | 15 | 13 | 14 | 10 | 12 | 8 |
| Bard | 8 | 14 | 12 | 13 | 10 | 15 |
| Cleric | 14 | 8 | 13 | 10 | 15 | 12 |
| Druid | 8 | 12 | 14 | 13 | 15 | 10 |
| Fighter | 15 | 14 | 13 | 8 | 10 | 12 |
| Monk | 12 | 15 | 13 | 10 | 14 | 8 |
| Paladin | 15 | 10 | 13 | 8 | 12 | 14 |
| Ranger | 12 | 15 | 13 | 8 | 14 | 10 |
| Rogue | 12 | 15 | 13 | 14 | 10 | 8 |
| Sorcerer | 10 | 13 | 14 | 8 | 12 | 15 |
| Warlock | 8 | 14 | 13 | 12 | 10 | 15 |
| Wizard | 8 | 12 | 13 | 15 | 14 | 10 |

## Method 2 — Random Roll

Roll **4d6, drop the lowest**, six times. Assign to abilities in any order.

## Method 3 — Point Buy

Spend **27 points** to buy scores from 8 to 15:

| Score | Cost |
| --- | --- |
| 8 | 0 |
| 9 | 1 |
| 10 | 2 |
| 11 | 3 |
| 12 | 4 |
| 13 | 5 |
| 14 | 7 |
| 15 | 9 |

[BR-2024 §01-creating-a-character#Step 3]

## Then apply background

After assignment, apply your [[character-origins|background]]'s **+2 / +1** (or **+1 / +1 / +1**). No score can exceed 20 from background bonuses.
