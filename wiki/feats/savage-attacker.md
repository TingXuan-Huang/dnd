# Savage Attacker

> Category: Origin · Prerequisites: None

Once per turn when you hit a target with a weapon, you can roll the weapon's damage dice twice and use either result. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Savage Attacker]

## Notes

- "Once per turn" — not once per round; resets at the start of each of your turns.
- Only the weapon's damage dice are rerolled; ability modifiers, extra dice from features, and bonus damage are unaffected.
