# Necromancy

> School of magic · Typical effects: Life and death

Necromancy spells manipulate the energies of life and death — wounding, healing, raising the dead, draining life. Basic Rules examples: *Inflict Wounds*, *Ray of Sickness*, *False Life*, *Chill Touch*. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Schools of Magic]
