# Repeating Shot

> **Weapon (any Simple or Martial with the [[ammunition]] property), Uncommon** · Requires Attunement · ⚠️ playtest (UA 2024 Artificer)

This magic weapon grants a **+1 bonus** to attack and damage rolls on ranged attacks, and it ignores the [[loading]] property if it has it. [UA 2024 Artificer]

If the weapon lacks ammunition, it **produces its own** — automatically creating one piece of magic ammunition when the wielder makes a ranged attack. The ammunition vanishes the instant after it hits or misses a target.
