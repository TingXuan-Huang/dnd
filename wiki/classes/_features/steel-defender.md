# Steel Defender

> [[battle-smith]] ([[artificer]] subclass) feature · Gained at level 3 · ⚠️ playtest

A construct ally created by the Battle Smith. Friendly to you and your allies, obeys you. Vanishes if you die. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## In combat

Acts on your turn. Can move and take its Reaction on its own, but its only Action is the **Dodge action** unless you spend a [[bonus-action]] to command it. If you have the [[incapacitated]] condition, it acts on its own and is no longer limited to Dodge.

## Restoring / replacing the defender

- **If dead within the last hour:** Magic action + spell slot → defender revives after 1 minute, full HP.
- **After a [[long-rest]]:** Create a new Steel Defender with Smith's Tools in hand. If you already have one, the old one vanishes.

## Stat block

> **Medium Construct** · **AC** 15 · **HP** 5 + 5 × your Artificer level · **Speed** 40 ft

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |

**Immunities:** Poison; Charmed, Exhaustion, Poisoned
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft, Passive Perception 10
**Languages:** Understands the languages you know
**CR:** None (XP 0; PB equals your Proficiency Bonus)

### Traits

- **Steel Bond.** Add your [[proficiency-bonus]] to any ability check or [[saving-throw]] the defender makes.

### Actions

- **Force-Empowered Rend.** *Melee Attack:* bonus = your spell attack mod, reach 5 ft. Hit: `1d8 + 2 + your Int modifier` Force damage.
- **Repair (3/day).** Heal the defender OR a Construct/object within 5 ft for `2d8 + your Int modifier` HP.

[UA 2024 Artificer]

## Upgrades

- **L15: Improved Defender.** +2 AC. When Steel Defender uses its Deflect Attack reaction, attacker takes 1d4 + Int Force damage.
