# Proficiency Bonus (PB)

A flat number you add to [[d20-test]]s where you have proficiency — with a skill, a save, an attack, or a tool. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Proficiency]

## Bonus by character level (or monster CR)

| Level or CR | Bonus |
| --- | --- |
| Up to 4 | +2 |
| 5–8 | +3 |
| 9–12 | +4 |
| 13–16 | +5 |
| 17–20 | +6 |

[BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Proficiency]

## Rules

- **Apply once per roll** — even if your proficiency would technically apply more than one way. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Proficiency]
- **[[expertise]]** doubles your Proficiency Bonus for the relevant skill or tool check.
