# Advantage and Disadvantage

When you have **Advantage** on a [[d20-test]], roll two d20s and use the higher result. When you have **Disadvantage**, roll two d20s and use the lower result. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Advantage and Disadvantage]

## Rules

- **They do not stack.** Having Advantage from two sources is the same as having it from one.
- **They cancel.** If you have both Advantage and Disadvantage on the same roll (from any sources), you roll a single d20 — neither applies. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Advantage and Disadvantage]
