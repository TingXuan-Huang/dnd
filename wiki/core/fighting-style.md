# Fighting Style

A class feature that grants one Fighting Style feat. [BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Fighter]

## Who gets it

- [[fighter]] at level 1
- [[paladin]] at a later level (per PHB 2024)
- [[ranger]] at a later level (per PHB 2024)

## Available Fighting Style feats (Basic Rules)

- [[archery]] — +2 to attack rolls with ranged weapons
- [[defense]] — +1 AC while wearing any armor
- [[great-weapon-fighting]] — Treat 1s and 2s as 3s on two-handed melee damage
- [[two-weapon-fighting]] — Add ability modifier to dual-wield extra-attack damage

The full PHB 2024 includes additional Fighting Style feats.
