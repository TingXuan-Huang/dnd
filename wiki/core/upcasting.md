# Upcasting

Casting a spell using a [[spell-slot]] higher than its base level. Some spells have an explicit "At Higher Levels" entry that scales their effect; others don't gain anything from being upcast except using a different slot. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Upcasting]

A higher-level slot can always cast a lower-level spell.
