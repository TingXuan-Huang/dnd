# Divination

> School of magic · Typical effects: Information

Divination spells reveal hidden information — locations, knowledge, glimpses of the future. Basic Rules examples: *Detect Magic*, *Detect Poison and Disease*, *Identify*, *Comprehend Languages*. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Schools of Magic]
