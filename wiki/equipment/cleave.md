# Cleave

> Weapon mastery

When you hit a creature with a melee [[attack-roll]] using this weapon, you can make a second attack with it against another creature within 5 feet of the first using the same attack roll. Once per turn. [BR-2024 §05-equipment#Weapon Mastery]
