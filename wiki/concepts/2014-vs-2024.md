# 2014 vs 2024 — Mixing Older Books

The 2024 revision uses a **background + species** origin model, distinct from the 2014 PHB's race + background pattern. When mixing 2014 content with 2024 Basic Rules, decide with your DM which ruleset takes precedence. [BR-2024 §08-supplements]

## What changed at a glance

| Topic | 2024 Basic Rules | 2014 PHB (typical) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Origin | [[character-origins\|Background + species]] | Race + background |
| ASIs at level 1 | Background + species options | Racial + class at L4/8/… |
| Feats at level 1 | Origin feat from background | Variant human, etc. |
| [[weapon-mastery]] | Class feature; new in 2024 | Did not exist |
| Spell lists | 2024 class lists | 2014 lists (overlap differs) |

[BR-2024 §08-supplements#2014 vs 2024]

## Practical guidance

- Pick D&D Beyond's character builder ruleset matching your table's choice (2014 Core vs 2024 Free Rules).
- Select class, background, and species from the **matching** rules section.
- **Do not stack** 2014 racial ASIs with 2024 background ASIs without DM approval. [BR-2024 §08-supplements#2014 vs 2024]
- **Subclass overlap.** If a 2014 subclass and a 2024 Basic Rules feature grant overlapping benefits, pick one option per benefit — don't double-dip.

## Mixing 2014 content

D&D Beyond still hosts the 2014 PHB's backgrounds, races, and subclasses separately from the 2024 Basic Rules set. Conversion guidance for 2014 backgrounds/species is in the "Backgrounds and Species from Older Books" sidebar on the [Creating a Character](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/creating-a-character) page.
