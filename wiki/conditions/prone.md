# Prone

While you have the Prone condition:

- **Restricted Movement.** Your only movement options are to **crawl** OR to spend movement equal to **half your [[speed]]** (round down) to right yourself, ending the condition. If your Speed is 0, you can't right yourself.
- **Attacks Affected.** You have [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on [[attack-roll|attack rolls]]. An attack roll against you has [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] if the attacker is within 5 feet, otherwise Disadvantage.

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#ProneCondition]

## Common sources

- [[unarmed-strike]] Shove option (target can fall Prone instead of being pushed)
- [[topple]] weapon mastery: target makes a Con save or falls Prone
- Falling damage — Prone unless you took no damage (e.g., feather-fall)
- Spells: *Earth Tremor*, *Grease* (failed save), *Sleep*
- Failed Long-Jump landing in Difficult Terrain (DC 10 Dex/Acrobatics)

## Notes

- **Tradeoff at range.** A Prone target is harder for *ranged* attacks (Disadv) but easier for *melee within 5 ft* (Adv). Decide whether knocking down helps based on the attacker's position.
- **Standing up is expensive.** Half your Speed. Don't get knocked down at low Speed.
