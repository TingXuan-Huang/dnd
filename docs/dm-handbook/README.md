# DM Handbook · Fallowton Campaign

Modular play files. Each module has four files: a play script, an NPC sheet, a checks catalog, and a one-page DM flow.

## Design philosophy

Each module is built around a **free / fixed** contract:

- **Free explore blocks** — players pick order, depth, what to skip. The DM provides options and lets the dice fall.
- **Fixed pivots** — story rails that must fire on time. The DM narrates these.

Together they give players the *feeling* of total agency while the DM keeps the session arc intact.

Every check is also classified:

- **Open** — players roll, results visible. Use for gates and visible outcomes.
- **Silent** — DM rolls or compares passives. Use for atmosphere, dread, and clue-pacing. On failure, narrate **nothing**.

The **Awakening Clock** is the only timer in Module 1. Hidden from players. Sets the stakes of the descent.

## Module 1 — From Village to Ruin

Status: **ready to run.** ~3.5 hours. Level 1, 3–5 PCs.

| File | What it is | When you open it |
|---|---|---|
| [module-1-preflight.md](module-1-preflight.md) | First-DM checklist — 24h-out prep, voice warm-up, verbatim opening, anti-patterns | **Morning of session** |
| [module-1-play.html](module-1-play.html) | The play script with bilingual read-aloud, marked FREE/FIXED beats | **During play** — read aloud from this |
| [module-1-npcs.html](module-1-npcs.html) | 7 major + 4 minor NPCs · stats · voices · secrets | **During play** — voice from this |
| [module-1-checks.md](module-1-checks.md) | Every DC in the module · open vs silent · fail-forward paths | **When a roll comes up** you don't remember |
| [module-1-dm-flow.md](module-1-dm-flow.md) | One-page cheat — clocks, pacing, cliffhanger triggers, log sheet | **Always open** during the session |

**End state of Module 1:** PCs back on surface with one of three cliffhangers (Mordren disappears / silver leaves / forgotten name). Awakening Clock at stage 1–3. Module 2 begins at sunrise with a tunnel of their choice.
