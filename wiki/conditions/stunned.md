# Stunned

While you have the Stunned condition:

- **[[incapacitated|Incapacitated]].** You have the Incapacitated condition.
- **Saving Throws Affected.** You automatically fail [[strength|Str]] and [[dexterity|Dex]] [[saving-throw|saves]].
- **Attacks Affected.** [[attack-roll|Attack rolls]] against you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]].

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#StunnedCondition]

## Common sources

- Monk **Stunning Strike** feature (Con save on a Martial Arts hit)
- Spells: *Power Word Stun*
- Various monster abilities

## Notes

- Stunned is **Paralyzed without the auto-crit-within-5-ft**. Otherwise nearly identical: Incapacitated + auto-fail Str/Dex + Advantage to attackers.
- The Speed reduction from Incapacitated only blocks action — Speed itself isn't 0 (unlike Paralyzed/Restrained), but you also can't take any action to use it.
