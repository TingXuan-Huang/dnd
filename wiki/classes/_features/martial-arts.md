# Martial Arts

> [[monk]] feature · Gained at level 1

While unarmed or wielding only monk weapons (Simple weapons + Martial weapons with the [[light]] property):

- **Martial Arts die:** d6 damage on unarmed strikes (scales with level).
- **Dex for unarmed/monk-weapon attacks:** Use [[dexterity]] for attack and damage rolls.
- **Bonus unarmed strike:** After taking the [[attack-action]], you can make one unarmed strike as a [[bonus-action]].
