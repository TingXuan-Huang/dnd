## Weave Strain Tracker

```
WEAVE STRAIN: ___ / 3        Purification: none | I | II | III | IV
Luck debt: ___

When I cast a leveled Druid spell:
  1. Cast (use slot)
  2. Strain +1
  3. Pick one cost from the strain table
  4. Note any villager reaction

At strain 3: NO more leveled spells until rest.
Short Rest: −1 strain  |  Long Rest: 0
```

**Background note:** As the herbalist's apprentice, your character *recognizes* Weave Strain symptoms in others — the grey look, the cold sweat, the missing word. You may be the one who notices when other PCs are pushing too hard. (DM may award you advantage on Medicine checks treating strain damage.)

**Healing & strain:** Cure Wounds and Healing Word will *each* cause +1 strain — mercy still has a price in this world.
