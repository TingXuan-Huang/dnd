# Illusion

> School of magic · Typical effects: Deception

Illusion spells deceive the senses — false images, sounds, scents, disguises. Basic Rules examples: *Disguise Self*, *Silent Image*, *Minor Illusion*, *Illusory Script*. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Schools of Magic]
