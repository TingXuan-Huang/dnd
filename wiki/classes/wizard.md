# Wizard

## Proficiencies

- **Weapons:** Simple
- **Armor:** None
- **Saves:** [[intelligence]], [[wisdom]]
- **Skills:** choose 2 of [[arcana]], [[history]], [[insight]], [[investigation]], [[medicine]], [[nature]], [[religion]]

## Starting equipment

- **A.** 2 Daggers, Arcane Focus (Quarterstaff), Robe, Spellbook, Scholar's Pack, 5 GP
- **B.** 55 GP

## Level 1 features

- [[spellcasting]] — Spellbook-based. Prepare (Int mod + wizard level) spells on long rest.
- [[ritual-adept]] — Cast ritual spells from your spellbook (no slot needed for ritual casting).
- [[arcane-recovery]] — Recover spell slots on a short rest (limited; scales with level).
