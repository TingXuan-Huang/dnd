***Poison Breath (Recharge 5–6).*** *Constitution Saving Throw:* DC 14, each creature in a 30-foot [Cone](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#ConeAreaofEffect). *Failure:* 42 (12d6) Poison damage. *Success:* Half damage.
