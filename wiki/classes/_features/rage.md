# Rage

> [[barbarian]] feature · Gained at level 1

As a [[bonus-action]], enter a rage while you aren't wearing Heavy Armor. While raging:

- **[[resistance]]** to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
- **Bonus damage** on Strength-based weapon attacks (Rage Damage; scales with level).
- **[[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]]** on [[strength]] checks and [[strength]] [[saving-throw|saves]].
- **Cannot cast spells or maintain [[concentration]].**

[BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Barbarian]

## Uses / recharge

- Uses per [[long-rest]] = your [[proficiency-bonus]].
- Regain 1 use on a short rest; all on a long rest.
