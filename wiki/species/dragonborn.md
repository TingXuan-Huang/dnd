# Dragonborn

> Size: Medium (5–7 ft) · Speed: 30 ft · Creature type: Humanoid

Descendants of dragons; you carry the breath weapon and resistance of your ancestor's lineage. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Dragonborn]

## Traits

- **[[darkvision]] 60 ft.** [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Dragonborn]
- **Draconic Ancestry.** Choose one dragon type at character creation; it determines the damage type for your Breath Weapon and Damage Resistance. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Dragonborn]
- **Breath Weapon.** As a Magic action, exhale destructive energy in a 15-foot cone or 30-foot line. Affected creatures make a [[dexterity]] [[saving-throw|save]]. Damage 1d10 (ancestry type); scales at level 5 (2d10), 11 (3d10), 17 (4d10). Uses = [[proficiency-bonus]] per [[long-rest]]. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Dragonborn]
- **Damage Resistance.** [[resistance]] to your ancestry's damage type. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Dragonborn]
- **Draconic Flight** *(Level 5+).* Grow wings and gain a fly speed equal to your Speed for 10 minutes. Once per [[long-rest]]. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Dragonborn]
