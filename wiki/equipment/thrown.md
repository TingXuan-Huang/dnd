# Thrown

> Weapon property

You can throw the weapon to make a ranged [[attack-roll]]. The attack uses the same ability modifier as a melee attack with the weapon: [[strength]] by default, [[dexterity]] if the weapon also has [[finesse]] and you so choose. The Thrown entry includes a range in feet, e.g., 20/60 ft. [BR-2024 §05-equipment#Weapon Properties]
