# Conjuration

> School of magic · Typical effects: Transport, summon

Conjuration spells move creatures or objects, summon them, or create them out of nothing. Basic Rules examples: *Find Familiar*, *Fog Cloud*, *Grease*, *Unseen Servant*. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Schools of Magic]
