# Monk

> Primary: [[dexterity]] and [[wisdom]] · Hit die: d8 · Saves: Strength, Dexterity

## Proficiencies

- **Weapons:** Simple and Martial weapons that have the [[light]] property
- **Armor:** None
- **Tools:** Choose one type of Artisan's Tools or Musical Instrument
- **Saves:** [[strength]], [[dexterity]]
- **Skills:** choose 2 of [[acrobatics]], [[athletics]], [[history]], [[insight]], [[religion]], [[stealth]]

## Starting equipment

- **A.** Spear, 5 Daggers, Artisan's Tools or Musical Instrument (chosen for tool proficiency), Explorer's Pack, 11 GP
- **B.** 50 GP

[BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Monk]

## Level 1 features

- [[martial-arts]] — d6 unarmed strike die; use Dex for unarmed strikes and monk weapons; Bonus Action unarmed strike after the [[attack-action]].
- [[unarmored-defense]] — Monk variant: AC = 10 + Dex + Wis (no shield).
