# Barbarian

> Primary: [[strength]] · Hit die: d12 · Saves: Strength, Constitution

## Proficiencies

- **Weapons:** Simple and Martial
- **Armor:** Light, Medium, Shields
- **Saves:** [[strength]], [[constitution]]
- **Skills:** choose 2 of [[animal-handling]], [[athletics]], [[intimidation]], [[nature]], [[perception]], [[survival]]

## Starting equipment

- **A.** Greataxe, 4 Handaxes, Explorer's Pack, 15 GP
- **B.** 75 GP

[BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Barbarian]

## Level 1 features

- [[rage]] — Bonus Action; resistance to bludgeoning/piercing/slashing damage; bonus damage on Strength attacks; advantage on Strength checks/saves; no spells or concentration. Uses = [[proficiency-bonus]], regain 1 on short rest, all on [[long-rest]].
- [[unarmored-defense]] — Barbarian variant: AC = 10 + Dex + Con (shield OK).
- [[weapon-mastery]] — Two melee weapon masteries; change one on long rest.
