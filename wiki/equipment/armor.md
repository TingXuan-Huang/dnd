## Armor training penalty

Without proficiency ("armor training") in a category, you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on [[strength]]- and [[dexterity]]-based [[d20-test]]s and **cannot cast spells** while wearing armor in that category. [BR-2024 §05-equipment#Armor]
