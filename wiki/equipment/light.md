# Light

> Weapon property

When you take the [[attack-action]] and attack with a Light weapon, you can make one additional attack as a [[bonus-action]] using another Light weapon you are wielding. You don't add your ability modifier to the bonus-action attack's damage unless the modifier is negative or you have a feature that says otherwise (see [[two-weapon-fighting]] feat / [[nick]] mastery).
