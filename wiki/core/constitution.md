# Constitution

> Measures: Health, stamina. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#The Six Abilities]

## Where Constitution applies

- **[[ability-check|Constitution checks]]:** enduring strain (running long distances, holding breath, going without sleep). Rare in play.
- **[[saving-throw|Constitution saves]]:** enduring toxins, harsh conditions, and maintaining [[concentration]] on spells.
- **Hit points:** Add your Constitution modifier to your hit-point total at every level.
- **Skills:** none.
