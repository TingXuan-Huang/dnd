# Petrified

While you have the Petrified condition:

- **Turned to Inanimate Substance.** You and any nonmagical objects you are wearing or carrying are transformed into a solid inanimate substance (usually stone). Your weight increases by a factor of 10, and you cease aging.
- **[[incapacitated|Incapacitated]].** You have the Incapacitated condition.
- **Speed 0.** Your [[speed]] is 0 and can't increase.
- **Attacks Affected.** [[attack-roll|Attack rolls]] against you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]].
- **Saving Throws Affected.** You automatically fail [[strength|Str]] and [[dexterity|Dex]] [[saving-throw|saves]].
- **Resist Damage.** [[resistance]] to all damage.
- **Poison Immunity.** Immunity to the [[poisoned]] condition.

## Common sources

- Basilisk / Medusa gaze
- *Flesh to Stone* spell
- Cockatrice bite (with multiple failed saves)
