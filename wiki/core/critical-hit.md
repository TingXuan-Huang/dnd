# Critical Hit

When you roll a natural 20 on an [[attack-roll]] (the d20 itself shows 20, regardless of modifiers), you score a critical hit. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Attack Rolls]

A critical hit:

- **Always hits**, regardless of the target's [[armor-class]].
- **Doubles damage dice.** Roll the attack's damage dice twice, then add modifiers normally. Extra dice from features (e.g., Sneak Attack, Smite) are also doubled.

The reverse — a natural 1 on an attack — is an **automatic miss**, regardless of modifiers.
