# Blinded

While you have the Blinded condition:

- **Can't See.** You can't see and automatically fail any [[ability-check]] that requires sight.
- **Attacks Affected.** [[attack-roll|Attack rolls]] against you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]]; your attack rolls have [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]].

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#BlindedCondition]

## Common sources

- A **Heavily Obscured** space — you have Blinded against anything inside it. [BR-2024 §rules-glossary#HeavilyObscured]
- Spells: *Blindness/Deafness*
- [[radiant-weapon]] reaction blinds the attacker on a failed Con save
