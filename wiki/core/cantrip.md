# Cantrip

A "level 0" spell. Cantrips can be cast at will and cost no [[spell-slot]]. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Casting Spells]

## Scaling

Cantrip damage typically scales with **total character level**, not class level, unless the spell's text says otherwise. Breakpoints are usually at character levels 5, 11, and 17.
