# Concentration

Some spells (and other effects) require you to **maintain concentration** while they remain active. You can concentrate on only one such effect at a time — starting a new concentration spell ends the previous one. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Concentration]

## How concentration ends

- You take damage and fail a [[constitution]] [[saving-throw|save]]: DC = 10, OR half the damage taken (whichever is higher).
- You voluntarily end it.
- You become [[incapacitated]] or die.
- The spell's duration expires.
- Another spell or effect explicitly ends it.
