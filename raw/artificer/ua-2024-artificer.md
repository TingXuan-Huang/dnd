ARTIFICER

A Magical Crafter and an Inventor of Wonders

Masters of invention, Artificers use ingenuity
and magic to unlock extraordinary capabilities in
objects. They see magic as a complex system
waiting to be decoded and then harnessed in
their spells and inventions. You can find
everything you need to play one of these
inventors in the next few sections.
