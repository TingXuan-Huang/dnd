# Pact Magic

> [[warlock]] feature · Gained at level 1

Warlock spellcasting works differently from other casters:

- **Few spell slots**, but they recover on a **short rest** (not just a long rest).
- All of your spell slots are the **same (highest) level** you can currently cast.
- Spellcasting ability: [[charisma]].
