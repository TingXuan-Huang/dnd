# Background: Sinkhole Witness

*Homebrew · Fallowton campaign · Formatted like D&D 2024 Basic Rules backgrounds*

Use this background if your character was in or near **Fallowton** when the north barley field collapsed, or if you grew up hearing that the old hills “remember things.”

---

## Sinkhole Witness

You worked Fallowton’s soil — barley, gray wheat, turnips — or you lived close enough to the north field that you were there when the ground gave way. You remember the sound before the sight: not a crash but a long, low groan, like something very old finally deciding to exhale. The pale stone below was too smooth, too patient, too unlike anything quarried within fifty miles.

The village elders said to stay away. The wizard said to pay attention.

You are caught between those voices, and you have not stopped thinking about the stairs.

**Ability Scores:** Strength, Constitution, Wisdom  

**Feat:** [Alert](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/feats#Alert) (you notice trouble before others—useful when the ruin “listens”)

## Optional personality prompts

- Who in Fallowton are you responsible for? Not "who do you like" — who would notice if you didn't come back?
- When the ground first opened: what was the first thought in your head? Not the fear. The thought *under* the fear.
- What detail about the buried stone has stayed with you: the stairs, the strange proportions, the blue seams, or the blank-faced carvings?  

## Weave Strain (if you gain magic later)

You do not start with strain unless you cast spells. If you become a spellcaster:

- Underground near the **House of First Light**, **ill luck** and **burnout** hit harder (+1 DC when casting at strain 3).  
- After casting, the DM may call for **disadvantage** on the next **Survival** or **Perception** check near the north field.
