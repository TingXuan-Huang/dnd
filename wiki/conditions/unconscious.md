# Unconscious

The "worst" condition — combines Incapacitated, Prone, and several auto-fails. While you have the Unconscious condition:

- **Inert.** You have the [[incapacitated|Incapacitated]] and [[prone|Prone]] conditions, and you drop whatever you're holding. When this condition ends, you **remain Prone**.
- **Speed 0.** Your [[speed]] is 0 and can't increase.
- **Attacks Affected.** [[attack-roll|Attack rolls]] against you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]].
- **Saving Throws Affected.** You automatically fail [[strength|Str]] and [[dexterity|Dex]] [[saving-throw|saves]].
- **Automatic Critical Hits.** Any attack roll that hits you is a [[critical-hit]] if the attacker is within 5 feet of you.
- **Unaware.** You're unaware of your surroundings.

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#UnconsciousCondition]

## Common sources

- Reaching 0 [[hit-points]] (for PCs, triggers [[death-saves]])
- Sleep during a [[long-rest]] (you are technically Unconscious while sleeping)
- Spells: *Sleep*
- **Knocking Out a Creature.** When you would reduce a creature to 0 HP with a melee attack, you can choose to reduce it to 1 HP and apply Unconscious instead; the creature starts a short rest and remains out until it regains HP or someone administers first aid (DC 10 Wis [[medicine]] check).
