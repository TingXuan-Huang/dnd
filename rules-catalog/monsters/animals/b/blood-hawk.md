Traits

***Pack Tactics.*** The hawk has Advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the hawk’s allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally doesn’t have the [Incapacitated](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#IncapacitatedCondition) condition.

Actions

***Beak.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +4, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) Piercing damage, or 6 (1d8 + 2) Piercing damage if the target is [Bloodied](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#Bloodied).
