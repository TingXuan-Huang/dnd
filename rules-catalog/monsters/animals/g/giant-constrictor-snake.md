Actions

***Multiattack.*** The snake makes one Bite attack and uses Constrict.

***Bite.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +6, reach 10 ft. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) Piercing damage.

***Constrict.*** *Strength Saving Throw:* DC 14, one Large or smaller creature the snake can see within 10 feet. *Failure:* 13 (2d8 + 4) Bludgeoning damage, and the target has the [Grappled](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#GrappledCondition) condition (escape DC 14).
