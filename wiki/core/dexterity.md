# Dexterity

> Measures: Agility, reflexes, balance. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#The Six Abilities]

## Where Dexterity applies

- **[[ability-check|Dexterity checks]]:** moving quietly, acrobatics, picking pockets.
- **[[saving-throw|Dexterity saves]]:** dodging area effects.
- **Ranged weapon [[attack-roll|attacks]]:** Dexterity provides the attack and damage modifier. Also applies to [[finesse]] melee weapons (your choice of Str or Dex).
- **[[armor-class]]:** base AC = 10 + Dex modifier.
- **Skills:** [[acrobatics]], [[sleight-of-hand]], [[stealth]].
