# Ritual Adept

> [[wizard]] feature · Gained at level 1

You can cast any spell you have in your spellbook as a **ritual**, with no spell slot consumed, provided the spell has the Ritual tag. Ritual casting adds 10 minutes to the casting time. [BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Wizard]
