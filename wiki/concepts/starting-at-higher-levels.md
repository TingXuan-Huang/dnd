# Starting at Higher Levels

If your campaign begins above level 1, characters start with extra gold and magic items to match the level. [BR-2024 §01-creating-a-character#Starting at Higher Levels]

## Wealth and magic items by starting level

| Starting level | Extra GP | Magic items |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 2–4 | Normal gear | 1 Common |
| 5–10 | 500 GP + 1d10 × 25 GP | 1 Common, 1 Uncommon |
| 11–16 | 5,000 GP + 1d10 × 250 GP | 2 Common, 3 Uncommon, 1 Rare |
| 17–20 | 20,000 GP + 1d10 × 250 GP | 2 Common, 4 Uncommon, 3 Rare, 1 Very Rare |

[BR-2024 §01-creating-a-character#Starting at Higher Levels]

## Setup notes

- Use the minimum XP for that level from the [[xp-and-advancement]] table.
- Choose subclass features for levels 3+ at creation.
