# Grappled

While you have the Grappled condition:

- **Speed 0.** Your [[speed]] is 0 and can't increase.
- **Attacks Affected.** [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on [[attack-roll|attack rolls]] against any target **other than the grappler**.
- **Movable.** The grappler can drag or carry you when it moves, but every foot of movement costs it 1 extra foot — unless you are Tiny or two or more sizes smaller than it.

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#GrappledCondition]

## How it starts

- An [[unarmed-strike]] using the Grapple option (target makes a Str OR Dex save).
- [[grappler]] feat's Punch-and-Grab combo.
- Monster grappling abilities.

## How it ends

- The grappled creature uses its action to make a [[strength|Str]] ([[athletics]]) OR [[dexterity|Dex]] ([[acrobatics]]) [[ability-check|check]] vs. the grapple's escape DC. **Success ends the condition.**
- The grappler becomes [[incapacitated]].
- The distance between target and grappler exceeds the grapple's range.
- The grappler releases the target (no action required).

## Notes

- One grapple per free hand (or designated body part for monsters).
- [[goliath]] **Powerful Build** grants [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on saves to end the Grappled condition.
