# Battle Smith

> [[artificer]] subclass · Available at level 3 · Source: UA 2024 Artificer (playtest)

A Battle Smith is a combination of protector and medic — an expert at defending others and repairing both materiel and personnel. Accompanied by a [[steel-defender]], a protective construct of their own creation. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Features by level

### L3: Tool Proficiency

Gain proficiency with **Smith's Tools** (or another Artisan's Tools if already proficient). Crafting an ordinary or magic weapon takes half the normal time.

### L3: Battle Ready

Two benefits:

- **Arcane Empowerment.** When you attack with a magic weapon, you may use [[intelligence]] (instead of Str/Dex) for the attack and damage rolls.
- **Weapon Knowledge.** Gain proficiency with Martial weapons.

### L3: Steel Defender

Create a [[steel-defender]] construct ally using Smith's Tools. See its stat block. It's friendly to you and your allies and obeys you; it vanishes if you die.

- **In combat:** acts on your turn. Default action is Dodge unless you take a [[bonus-action]] to command it.
- **Restoring/replacing:** if dead within last hour, touch it with a Magic action + spell slot to revive after 1 minute at full HP.
- **New defender:** after a [[long-rest]] with Smith's Tools, create a new one (replacing the old if any).

### L9: Arcane Jolt

When you hit with a magic weapon OR your [[steel-defender]] hits, channel magic for one of:

- **Destructive Energy.** Target takes +2d6 Force damage.
- **Restorative Energy.** Choose any creature/object within 30 ft of the target; heal it 2d6 HP.

Uses per [[long-rest]] = your [[intelligence]] modifier. Max once per turn.

### L15: Improved Defender

- **Improved Jolt.** Arcane Jolt damage and healing increase to 4d6.
- **Fortified Defender.** Steel Defender gains +2 AC.
- **Improved Deflection.** When the defender uses Deflect Attack (reaction), attacker takes 1d4 + Int Force damage.
