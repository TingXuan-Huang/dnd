# Incapacitated

A "base" condition that several others compound. While you have the Incapacitated condition:

- **Inactive.** You can't take any Action, [[bonus-action|Bonus Action]], or [[reaction|Reaction]].
- **No Concentration.** Your [[concentration]] is broken.
- **Speechless.** You can't speak.
- **Surprised.** If you're Incapacitated when you roll [[initiative]], you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on the roll.

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#IncapacitatedCondition]

## Conditions that include Incapacitated

These all compound — applying any of them automatically applies Incapacitated:

- [[paralyzed]]
- [[petrified]]
- [[stunned]]
- [[unconscious]]

## Common sources

- Spells: *Tasha's Hideous Laughter* (causes Prone and Incapacitated)
- Monster abilities
