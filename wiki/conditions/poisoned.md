# Poisoned

While you have the Poisoned condition:

- **Ability Checks and Attacks Affected.** [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on [[attack-roll|attack rolls]] and [[ability-check|ability checks]].

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#PoisonedCondition]

## Common sources

- Various poisons (Basic Poison, drow Poison, etc. — see DMG 2024 for the full list)
- Disease (some)
- Spider/snake bites and other natural-poison monster attacks
- Spells: *Ray of Sickness*, *Cloudkill* (failed save)

## Defenses

- [[dwarf]] **Dwarven Resilience** grants [[resistance]] to poison damage and [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on saves to avoid being Poisoned.
- A [[petrified]] creature is **immune** to being Poisoned.
- *Protection from Poison* spell — Advantage on saves, ends current Poisoned.
