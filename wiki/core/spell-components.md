# Spell Components

Most spells require one or more components to cast. A spell's entry lists which it needs. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Components]

## The three components

- **V (Verbal).** Spoken words. Blocked if you are gagged or in an area of magical silence.
- **S (Somatic).** Hand gestures. Requires a free hand.
- **M (Material).** Physical objects. A component pouch or spell focus substitutes for any non-consumed, no-cost components. Consumed or expensive components must be supplied individually.
