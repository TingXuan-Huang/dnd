# Reach

> Weapon property

This melee weapon's reach is 5 feet greater than normal — you can attack targets up to 10 feet away (instead of 5 ft). The added reach also applies to [[opportunity-attack]]s. [BR-2024 §05-equipment#Weapon Properties]
