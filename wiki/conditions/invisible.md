# Invisible

While you have the Invisible condition:

- **Surprise.** If you're Invisible when you roll [[initiative]], you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on the roll.
- **Concealed.** You aren't affected by any effect that requires its target to be seen, unless the effect's creator can somehow see you. Worn/carried equipment is also concealed.
- **Attacks Affected.** [[attack-roll|Attack rolls]] against you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]]; your attack rolls have [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]]. A creature that can see you (e.g., via [[truesight]]) negates this benefit.

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#InvisibleCondition]

## How it starts

- Successful [[hide-action]] — DC 15 Dex ([[stealth]]) check while Heavily Obscured or behind cover.
- Spells: *Invisibility*, *Greater Invisibility*, *Pass without Trace* (mechanic varies)
- Some species/monsters

## How it ends

- You make an attack roll, cast a spell with a Verbal component, or make a sound louder than a whisper (for the [[hide-action]] case).
- The spell's duration expires.
- An enemy finds you (Perception check beats your hidden DC).

## Counters

- [[truesight]] sees through invisibility.
- *See Invisibility* spell.
- Blindsight (within range) detects regardless.
