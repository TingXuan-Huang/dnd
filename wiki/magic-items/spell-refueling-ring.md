# Spell-Refueling Ring

> **Ring, Uncommon** · Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster · ⚠️ playtest (UA 2024 Artificer)

While wearing this ring, you can recover **one expended [[spell-slot]]** as a [[bonus-action]]. The recovered slot can be of **level 3 or lower**. Once used, the ring can't be used again until the next dawn. [UA 2024 Artificer]
