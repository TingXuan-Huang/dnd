# Ritual Casting

Cast a spell as a ritual: no [[spell-slot]] is expended, but the casting time is **10 minutes longer** than normal. The spell must be tagged as a Ritual, and you must normally have it prepared (or have a class feature that lets you ritual-cast from a book — see [[ritual-adept]] for the Wizard).
