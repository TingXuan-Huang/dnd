# Versatile

> Weapon property

The weapon can be wielded one-handed or two-handed. The damage when wielded two-handed is shown in parentheses on the weapon entry (e.g., Quarterstaff: 1d6 / 1d8). [BR-2024 §05-equipment#Weapon Properties]
