# Slow

> Weapon mastery

When you hit a creature with this weapon, its Speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn. Multiple Slow hits on the same target don't stack — its Speed can't be reduced beyond the original −10 ft. [BR-2024 §05-equipment#Weapon Mastery]
