# Heavy

> Weapon property

You have [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on [[attack-roll]]s with this weapon if your [[strength]] is below 13 (for a melee Heavy weapon) or your [[dexterity]] is below 13 (for a ranged Heavy weapon). [BR-2024 §05-equipment#Weapon Properties]
