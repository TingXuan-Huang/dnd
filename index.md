## Core mechanics

**D20 system**
- [[d20-test]] — Umbrella for ability checks, saves, attack rolls.
- [[ability-check]] — Test against a Difficulty Class.
- [[saving-throw]] — Test to resist a hazard.
- [[attack-roll]] — Test to hit; compare to AC.
- [[critical-hit]] — Natural 20: hit + doubled damage dice.
- [[advantage-disadvantage]] — Roll 2d20, take higher/lower; do not stack; cancel each other.
- [[difficulty-class]] — Target number scale (DC 5 very easy → 30 nearly impossible).
- [[armor-class]] — Target number to hit a creature.
- [[proficiency-bonus]] — +2 to +6 by level; applied once per roll where proficient.
- [[expertise]] — Double proficiency bonus on a skill/tool check.

**Abilities**
- [[ability-scores]] — The six scores, modifier table, scale 1–30.
- [[strength]] · [[dexterity]] · [[constitution]] · [[intelligence]] · [[wisdom]] · [[charisma]]

**Skills (18)** — see [[skills-overview]] for master table.
- *Strength:* [[athletics]]
- *Dexterity:* [[acrobatics]], [[sleight-of-hand]], [[stealth]]
- *Constitution:* (none)
- *Intelligence:* [[arcana]], [[history]], [[investigation]], [[nature]], [[religion]]
- *Wisdom:* [[animal-handling]], [[insight]], [[medicine]], [[perception]], [[survival]]
- *Charisma:* [[deception]], [[intimidation]], [[performance]], [[persuasion]]

**Class systems** (referenced by multiple classes)
- [[spellcasting]] — General framework; per-class variations.
- [[weapon-mastery]] — New in 2024; martial classes gain mastery slots.
- [[weapon-properties]] — 10 properties (Finesse, Heavy, Light, Reach, etc.).
- [[fighting-style]] — Class feature granting one Fighting Style feat.

**Survival & combat math**
- [[hit-points]] — HP at level 1 and per level, by class group.
- [[initiative]] — Dex-based d20 test at start of combat.
- [[passive-perception]] — 10 + Perception modifier.

**Spellcasting machinery**
- [[cantrip]] — Level 0 spell; cast at will.
- [[spell-slot]] — Container for casting leveled spells.
- [[spell-preparation]] — When and how many spells you can change.
- [[ritual-casting]] — No slot; +10 min.
- [[upcasting]] — Use higher slots for lower-level spells.
- [[spell-components]] — Verbal, Somatic, Material.
- [[concentration]] — Maintain active spells; Con save when damaged.

## Classes

- [[artificer]] — d8 · INT · half-caster. **⚠️ playtest** (UA 2024). Magical Tinkering; Replicate Magic Item.
- [[barbarian]] — d12 · STR · martial. Rage; weapon mastery (2); unarmored defense.
- [[bard]] — d8 · CHA · full caster. Bardic inspiration; instrument as focus.
- [[cleric]] — d8 · WIS · full caster. Divine order (Protector / Thaumaturge).
- [[druid]] — d8 · WIS · full caster. Primal order (Magician / Warden); wild shape at L2.
- [[fighter]] — d10 · STR or DEX · martial. Fighting style + second wind + 3 weapon masteries.
- [[monk]] — d8 · DEX+WIS · martial. Martial arts; unarmored defense.
- [[paladin]] — d10 · STR+CHA · half caster. Lay on hands; divine sense; 2 weapon masteries.
- [[ranger]] — d10 · DEX+WIS · half caster. Favored enemy; 2 weapon masteries.
- [[rogue]] — d8 · DEX · skill-monkey. Expertise + sneak attack + thieves' cant + 2 masteries.
- [[sorcerer]] — d6 · CHA · full caster. Innate sorcery; sorcery points at L2.
- [[warlock]] — d8 · CHA · pact caster. Pact magic (short-rest slots) + eldritch invocations.
- [[wizard]] — d6 · INT · full caster. Spellbook; ritual adept; arcane recovery.

## Feats

**Origin** (taken at level 1 via background)
- [[alert]] — Add proficiency to initiative; swap initiative with willing ally.
- [[magic-initiate]] — 2 cantrips + 1 level-1 spell from Cleric/Druid/Wizard. Repeatable.
- [[savage-attacker]] — Once/turn, roll weapon damage dice twice; take either.
- [[skilled]] — Proficiency in any 3 skills or tools. Repeatable.

**General** (require level 4+)
- [[ability-score-improvement]] — +2 to one ability or +1 to two (max 20). Repeatable.
- [[grappler]] — Punch+Grab combo; advantage on grappled targets. Str/Dex 13+.

**Fighting Style** (require Fighting Style class feature)
- [[archery]] — +2 to attack rolls with ranged weapons.
- [[defense]] — +1 AC while wearing any armor.
- [[great-weapon-fighting]] — Treat 1s and 2s as 3s on two-handed melee damage.
- [[two-weapon-fighting]] — Add ability modifier to dual-wield extra attack damage.

**Epic Boon** (require level 19+; each grants +1 ability, max 30)
- [[boon-of-combat-prowess]] — Turn a miss into a hit, once/turn.
- [[boon-of-dimensional-travel]] — After Attack/Magic action, teleport 30 ft.
- [[boon-of-fate]] — Reaction: ±2d4 to any d20 test within 60 ft.
- [[boon-of-irresistible-offense]] — B/P/S ignores resistance; crits deal extra damage.
- [[boon-of-spell-recall]] — On level 1–4 cast, 1d4 chance slot is not spent.
- [[boon-of-the-night-spirit]] — Hide in dim/dark; resist all damage except psychic/radiant.
- [[boon-of-truesight]] — Truesight 60 ft.

## Conditions

The 15 official conditions from BR-2024 Rules Glossary. See [[conditions-overview]] for general rules and the composition map.

- [[blinded]] — Auto-fail sight checks; Disadv on your attacks, Adv on attacks against you.
- [[charmed]] — Can't harm charmer; charmer Adv on social checks.
- [[deafened]] — Auto-fail hearing checks.
- [[exhaustion]] — Cumulative 1–6 levels; −2 per level on d20 tests; −5 ft Speed per level; die at 6.
- [[frightened]] — Disadv on checks/attacks while source visible; can't approach.
- [[grappled]] — Speed 0; Disadv on attacks against anyone but the grappler.
- [[incapacitated]] — No actions/bonus/reactions; no concentration; speechless.
- [[invisible]] — Adv on Initiative; concealed; Adv on your attacks, Disadv on attacks against.
- [[paralyzed]] — Incapacitated + Speed 0 + auto-fail Str/Dex + auto-crit within 5 ft.
- [[petrified]] — Turned to stone; Incapacitated; Resistance to all damage; immune to Poisoned.
- [[poisoned]] — Disadv on attacks and ability checks.
- [[prone]] — Half-speed to stand; Disadv on attacks; Adv against within 5 ft.
- [[restrained]] — Speed 0; Adv against; Disadv from; Disadv on Dex saves.
- [[stunned]] — Incapacitated + auto-fail Str/Dex + Adv against.
- [[unconscious]] — Incapacitated + Prone + auto-crit within 5 ft + unaware.
