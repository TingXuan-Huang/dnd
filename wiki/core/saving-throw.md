# Saving Throw

A [[d20-test]] made to resist a hazard — fire, poison, a spell, a mental attack. Add your [[proficiency-bonus]] if you are proficient in that save. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Saving Throws]

## Typical save by ability

| Ability | Typical save |
| --- | --- |
| [[strength]] | Resist being moved or forced |
| [[dexterity]] | Dodge area effects |
| [[constitution]] | Endure toxins, harsh conditions |
| [[intelligence]] | Disbelieve illusions, resist mental attacks |
| [[wisdom]] | Resist mental influence (charm, fear) |
| [[charisma]] | Assert identity (resist transform/possess/banish) |
