## Quick reference

- **Village:** Fallowton (“fallow” = sleeping land; ordinary name, hidden darkness)
- **Temple / Converter (old name):** The House of First Light
- **Entrance:** Sinkhole in the north barley field → black stone stairs → collapsed central hall → **three tunnels** (not doors)
- **Living ruin:** Half-dead, half-dreaming; Dawnweave protections decayed into instinct (see ruin clock in Module 2)
- **Wizard:** Mordren Vale — true scholar; hiding his Converter thesis and possible personal failures **【DM DECISION: past failure】**

## Three tunnels (symbolic corruption)

| Tunnel | Phenomenon | Theme | Primary reward |
|--------|------------|-------|----------------|
| Blue Light | Tiny blue motes; broken dagger at entrance | Duty corrupted — protectors who no longer know whom to defend | Ancient gear (recommended: **Lantern of First Light**) |
| Weird Sounds | Chittering, scraping, wet footsteps | Hunger corrupted — scavengers defiling the sacred | Gold (dirty; village theft evidence) |
| Empty Wind | Cold hollow wind; too quiet | Knowledge corrupted — protections broken and dangerous | **Dawnweave Map** |

## Recommended first session

1. Surface: sinkhole, village debate, hooks  
2. Descent: carvings defaced → ruined central chamber  
3. **One tunnel** explored (players rarely clear all three in one session)  
4. Return or cliffhanger; advance **Ruin Awakening Clock**  
5. Fallowton reaction + campaign hook  

**Suggested first build-out:** Blue Light tunnel (strongest mythic tone + Dawnweave lore). **【DM DECISION: which tunnel to detail first】**
