# Experience and Advancement

Characters earn XP and level up as they overcome challenges. [BR-2024 §01-creating-a-character#Level Advancement]

## Levels and proficiency bonus

| Level | XP needed | [[proficiency-bonus]] |
| --- | --- | --- |

| 2 | 300 | +2 |

| 4 | 2,700 | +2 |
| 5 | 6,500 | +3 |
| 10 | 64,000 | +4 |
| 15 | 165,000 | +5 |
| 20 | 355,000 | +6 |

[BR-2024 §01-creating-a-character#Level Advancement]

## What you gain at each level

- HP increase — roll your class's hit die or take the fixed average. See [[hit-points]].
- Class features per the class's level table.
- At certain levels: an Ability Score Improvement or a feat (see [[ability-score-improvement]]).
- Subclass features starting at level 3.
