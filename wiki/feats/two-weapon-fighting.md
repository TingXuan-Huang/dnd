# Two-Weapon Fighting

> Category: Fighting Style · Prerequisites: Fighting Style class feature

When you engage in two-weapon fighting, you can add your ability modifier to the damage of the extra attack with a [[light]] weapon, even if you would not normally do so. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Two-Weapon Fighting]
