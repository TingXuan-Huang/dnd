# Restrained

While you have the Restrained condition:

- **Speed 0.** Your [[speed]] is 0 and can't increase.
- **Attacks Affected.** [[attack-roll|Attack rolls]] against you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]]; your attack rolls have [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]].
- **Saving Throws Affected.** [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on [[dexterity|Dex]] [[saving-throw|saves]].

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#RestrainedCondition]

## Common sources

- Spells: *Entangle*, *Web*, *Ensnaring Strike*
- Net (an improvised ranged weapon)
- Monster abilities: Roper tendrils, Giant Spider web, etc.

## Notes

- **Restrained > Grappled.** Restrained adds disadvantage on your attacks AND disadvantage on Dex saves on top of the Speed-0 and attack-vulnerability that Grappled also has.
- Restrained does NOT automatically make you Prone (compare to Web/Entangle, which may add Prone separately if the spell says so).
