# Boots of the Winding Path

> **Wondrous Item, Uncommon** · Requires Attunement · ⚠️ playtest (UA 2024 Artificer)

While wearing these boots, you can take a [[bonus-action]] to teleport up to 15 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. You must have **occupied that space at some point during the current turn**. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Notes

- An [[artificer]] can craft this via [[replicate-magic-item]] at level 2+.
