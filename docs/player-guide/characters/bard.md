# [Bard — Priscilla] · Player Sheet

**Class:** Bard · **Level:** 1
**Species:** Wood Elf · **Background:** *(verify — sheet shows Alert origin feat)*
**Alignment:** Lawful Good · **Pronouns:** She/Her  
**Personality:** Cooperative, Compassionate

> *Narrative origin:* Left as an infant on the doorstep of a Fallowton tavern, raised by its kindhearted keeper. Grew up running trays, singing for travelers, and slipping out the back door to roam the woods — fox dens, streambanks, the small living things most people walk past. Apprenticed under the town apothecary, learning roots, poultices, and the difference between a remedy and a poison. Curious, cheerful, and quietly restless: knows every story the tavern's travelers ever told, and means to go see for themselves where those roads lead.

## Class Features (L1)

- **Bardic Inspiration** — Bonus Action: give one creature within 60 ft a **d6**. They can add it to one ability check, attack roll, or saving throw within 10 minutes. Use **2 times** per Long Rest. *(Switches to Short Rest recovery at L5.)*
- **Spellcasting** — see Spellcasting block

## Species Features (Wood Elf)

- **Fey Ancestry** — advantage on saving throws to avoid or end the Charmed condition
- **Keen Senses** — proficiency in Perception, Insight, or Survival *(included above)*
- **Trance** — finish a Long Rest in 4 hours of meditation; gain proficiency in two weapons of choice OR add proficiency bonus to one tool of choice
- **Elven Lineage (Wood Elf)** — Speed becomes 35 ft; learn **Druidcraft** cantrip; at higher levels gain *Longstrider* and *Pass Without Trace*
- **Languages:** Common, Elvish, + 1 other (Halfling chosen)

## Origin Feat

- **Alert** — Initiative bonus +PB; cannot be surprised while conscious; swap initiative with a willing ally
