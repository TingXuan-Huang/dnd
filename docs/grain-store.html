The Grain Store

Communal barn · barley · rye · gray wheat · shared village reserve

Floor Plan — The Grain Store · Upper loft shown dashed

The barn smells of dust, dry grain, and the sweetness of cut straw. High above you, the loft groans faintly in the wind. The sacks along the walls are fewer than they should be at this time of year — noticeably so. Someone has drawn a tally on the eastern wall post in chalk: the numbers are not good.

Who's Here

Tomm Ashfield
Grain warden · Bessa's cousin · worried · keeps meticulous ledgers
Tomm has been grain warden for twelve years. He is calm about most things but not about the current reserves. The harvest was poor before the sinkhole, and since it opened, three farmers have refused to bring their grain in — they don't want to walk past the north field. He has put the numbers in the ledger and not told Elder Orwick yet. He is building up to it.

Notable Details

  • Current stores: roughly 60% of what this time of year should hold. Tomm's tally shows which farms have not contributed and why.
  • The loft is accessible by ladder. It holds loose straw, older cracked grain sacks, and three broken tools that have been "stored" rather than repaired for years.
  • Something has been in the grain. Two sacks in the west row have been gnawed through — but the holes are too large for rats, and there are no rat droppings. Tomm has not investigated.
  • The ledger desk holds detailed records going back forty years. The oldest entries are in a different hand and use a slightly different calendar system — off by three days.
  • A set of carved wooden measures on the south shelf predate Fallowton's founding. They use units that no one currently knows.

DM Only

The gnawed sacks were chewed from inside by Nail-Eater goblins who entered through a drain channel on the north side of the barn — the same drainage channel that connects, eventually, to the old underground water system below the hill. They are not living here peacefully; this was a surface raid made possible by weakened earth seals after the sinkhole opened. By default there are no four-goblin surface fights here. If you want a small complication, place one frightened Goblin Minion in the loft: it flees north toward the cave if discovered. The old measures on the south shelf are in Dawnweave standard units. A character with Arcana DC 12 or History DC 10 can identify them as pre-calendar. With a 16+, they can determine they match the unit system used in the carvings in the cave.
