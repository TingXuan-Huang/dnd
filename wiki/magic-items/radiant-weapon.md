# Radiant Weapon

> **Weapon (any), Uncommon** · Requires Attunement · ⚠️ playtest (UA 2024 Artificer)

This magic weapon grants a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with it. While wielded, you can:

- **Emit light** as a [[bonus-action]] — Bright Light 30 ft, Dim Light for an additional 30 ft. Extinguish as a bonus action.
- **Blinding reaction** — When you are hit by an [[attack-roll]], take a Reaction and expend **1 of the weapon's 4 charges** to force the attacker to make a **DC 15 Constitution save**. On a failure, the attacker has the [[blinded]] condition until the end of its next turn.

The weapon regains **1d4** expended charges daily at dawn. [UA 2024 Artificer]
