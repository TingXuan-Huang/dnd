# Gnome

> Size: Small (3–4 ft) · Speed: 30 ft · Lifespan: ~425 years · Creature type: Humanoid

[BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Gnome]

## Traits

- **[[darkvision]] 60 ft.** [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Gnome]
- **Gnomish Cunning.** [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on [[intelligence]], [[wisdom]], and [[charisma]] [[saving-throw|saves]]. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Gnome]
- **Gnome Subrace.** Choose Forest Gnome or Rock Gnome (see below).

## Subraces

- **Forest Gnome.** Know the *Minor Illusion* cantrip. Cast *Speak with Animals* without a spell slot, [[proficiency-bonus]] times per [[long-rest]]. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Gnome]
- **Rock Gnome.** Know the *Mending* and *Prestidigitation* cantrips. Use *Prestidigitation* to construct tiny clockwork devices. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Gnome]
