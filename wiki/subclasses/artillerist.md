# Artillerist

> [[artificer]] subclass · Available at level 3 · Source: UA 2024 Artificer (playtest)

An Artillerist specializes in using magic to hurl energy, projectiles, and explosions across a battlefield. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Features by level

### L3: Tool Proficiency

Gain proficiency with **Woodcarver's Tools** (or another Artisan's Tools if already proficient). Crafting a magic Wand takes half the normal time.

### L3: Eldritch Cannon

Using Woodcarver's Tools or Smith's Tools, take a Magic action to create a Small or Tiny [[eldritch-cannon]] in an unoccupied space within 5 feet. See [[eldritch-cannon]] for the full stat block.

You can have only one cannon at a time. Once created, you cannot create another until you finish a [[long-rest]] OR expend a spell slot to create one. Cannon disappears at 0 HP or after 1 hour. You can dismiss it early as a Magic action.

### L5: Arcane Firearm

After a [[long-rest]], use Woodcarver's Tools to carve sigils into a Rod, Staff, or Wand — making it your **arcane firearm**. The sigils transfer when you carve a new firearm.

- Usable as a [[spellcasting|spellcasting focus]] for Artificer spells.
- When you cast an Artificer spell through the firearm, roll **1d8** and add the result to one of the spell's damage rolls.

### L9: Explosive Cannon

Your [[eldritch-cannon]] gains:

- **Detonate.** When your cannon drops to 0 HP, as a Reaction (if within 60 ft), make it explode: every creature within 20 ft makes a Dex save vs. your spell DC, 3d10 Force damage (half on success). Destroys the cannon.
- **Firepower.** Cannon damage rolls increase by +1d8.

### L15: Fortified Position

- **Double Firepower.** Have **two cannons** simultaneously. Create both with one Magic action (but not via the same spell slot). Activate both with one Bonus Action.
- **Shimmering Field Projection.** You and allies have Half [[cover]] while within 10 ft of your cannon.
