# Armorer

> [[artificer]] subclass · Available at level 3 · Source: UA 2024 Artificer (playtest)

An Armorer modifies a suit of armor to function as a magical second skin — honing the wearer's magic, unleashing potent attacks, and providing formidable defense. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Features by level

### L3: Tool Proficiency

Gain training with **Heavy armor** and proficiency with **Smith's Tools** (or another Artisan's Tools if already proficient). Crafting nonmagical or magic armor takes half the normal time.

### L3: Arcane Armor

As a Magic action while holding Smith's Tools, turn a suit of armor you're wearing into **Arcane Armor**. It stays Arcane Armor until you don other armor or die.

While wearing Arcane Armor:

- **No Strength requirement** for the armor.
- **Quick Don/Doff.** Don or doff as a Utilize action.
- **Second Skin.** Attaches to you; can't be removed against your will. Covers your entire body (helmet retractable as a [[bonus-action]]). Replaces missing limbs.
- **Spellcasting Focus.** You can use Arcane Armor as your Artificer spellcasting focus.

### L3: Armor Model

Choose one of three models when you create your Arcane Armor (changeable on long rest). Each grants a special weapon and other features. When you attack with the model's weapon, you can add your [[intelligence]] modifier (instead of Str/Dex) to the attack and damage rolls.

#### Dreadnought

- **Armor Flail.** Simple Melee weapon, 1d10 Bludgeoning + ability mod. [[reach]].
- **Giant Stature.** As a [[bonus-action]], grow to Large size for 1 minute; reach +5 ft. PB uses per long rest.
- **Wrecking Ball.** On a hit with the flail against a smaller creature, push or pull it up to 10 feet.

#### Guardian

- **Thunder Gauntlets.** Simple Melee weapon, 1d8 Thunder + ability mod. Each gauntlet imposes Disadvantage on the target's attacks against anyone but you (until your next turn).
- **Defensive Field.** While you're Bloodied, as a [[bonus-action]] gain Temp HP = your Artificer level. Lost if you doff the armor.

#### Infiltrator

- **Lightning Launcher.** Simple Ranged weapon, 1d6 Lightning + ability mod. [[thrown]] (90/300 ft).
- **Lightning Jolt.** Once per turn on a Lightning Launcher hit, deal +1d6 Lightning damage.
- **Powered Steps.** Speed +5 ft.
- **Dampening Field.** [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on [[dexterity|Dex]] ([[stealth]]) checks. Cancels armor Stealth disadvantage.

[UA 2024 Artificer]

### L5: Extra Attack

Attack twice instead of once with the Attack action.

### L9: Armor Replication

Learn an additional plan for [[replicate-magic-item]], which must be in the Armor category. You can have one additional replicated Armor item active.

### L15: Perfected Armor

Your Arcane Armor gains a model-specific boost:

- **Dreadnought.** Armor Flail damage 2d6 Bludgeoning. Giant Stature reach +10 ft, can grow to Huge, gain fly speed.
- **Guardian.** Reaction: pull a Huge-or-smaller creature within 30 ft up to 25 ft toward you (Str save). If pulled within 5 ft, free melee attack.
- **Infiltrator.** Lightning Launcher damage 2d6. Targets struck glimmer with light, shedding 5-ft Dim Light and having Disadvantage on attacks against you until your next turn.
