# Repulsion Shield

> **Armor (Shield), Uncommon** · No Attunement · ⚠️ playtest (UA 2024 Artificer)

You gain a **+1 bonus to AC** while wielding this shield. [UA 2024 Artificer]

The shield has **4 charges**. While holding it, you can take a Reaction immediately after being hit by a melee [[attack-roll]] to expend 1 charge and **push the attacker up to 15 feet away**. The shield regains **1d4** expended charges daily at dawn.
