# Spell-Storing Item

> [[artificer]] feature · Gained at level 11 · ⚠️ playtest

Whenever you finish a [[long-rest]], touch one Simple/Martial weapon OR one item you can use as a [[spellcasting]] focus, and store a level **1, 2, or 3** Artificer spell in it (action casting time required; you don't need it prepared). [UA 2024 Artificer]

## How it works

- While holding the object, **any creature** can take an action to release the spell's effect from it, using your spell save DC/attack bonus.
- If the spell requires [[concentration]], the user (not you) concentrates on it.
- The spell stays in the object until used **2 × your Intelligence modifier** times (minimum 2) — or until you store a new spell.

## Change from TCoE

Previously level 1 or 2 spells only. UA 2024 raises the cap to **level 3**.
