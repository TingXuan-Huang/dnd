# Investigation

> Ability: [[intelligence]]

Deducing the location of a hidden object, discerning from the appearance of a wound what weapon dealt it, or finding the weakest point in a tunnel. Reasoning from clues, as distinct from [[perception]] (using the senses). [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Skills]
