# Magical Tinkering

> [[artificer]] feature · Gained at level 1 · ⚠️ playtest

As a Magic action while holding **Tinker's Tools**, create one item in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of yourself, chosen from a list of mundane objects (e.g., Ball Bearings, Bedroll, Candle, Crowbar, Lamp, Rope, Tinderbox, Torch, Vial, etc.). The item vanishes after **1 hour**. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Uses

- Uses per [[long-rest]] = your [[intelligence]] modifier (minimum 1).

## At level 3: Right Tool for the Job

The list of items you can create expands to include **Artisan's Tools**. [UA 2024 Artificer]
