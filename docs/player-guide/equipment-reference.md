# Equipment Reference (2024 Basic Rules)

*Appendix for the Fallowton player guide · Prices in **gp** unless noted*

Official source: [Equipment — D&D Beyond 2024](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/equipment)

When your class or background lists a **pack**, you get everything in that pack. You can also buy items separately with starting gold.

## Armor (wear only if you have **armor training**)

| Armor | AC | Training | Weight | Cost |
|-------|-----|----------|--------|------|
| [Leather](https://www.dndbeyond.com/equipment/10-leather) | 11 + Dex | Light | 10 lb | 10 GP |
| [Studded leather](https://www.dndbeyond.com/equipment/3-studded-leather) | 12 + Dex | Light | 13 lb | 45 GP |
| [Hide](https://www.dndbeyond.com/equipment/11-hide) | 12 + Dex (max 2) | Medium | 12 lb | 10 GP |
| [Chain shirt](https://www.dndbeyond.com/equipment/12-chain-shirt) | 13 + Dex (max 2) | Medium | 20 lb | 50 GP |
| [Scale mail](https://www.dndbeyond.com/equipment/14-scale-mail) | 14 + Dex (max 2) | Medium | 45 lb | 50 GP |
| [Chain mail](https://www.dndbeyond.com/equipment/16-chain-mail) | 16 | Heavy | 55 lb | 75 GP |
| [Shield](https://www.dndbeyond.com/equipment/8-shield) | +2 | Shields | 6 lb | 10 GP |

**Without armor:** AC = 10 + Dexterity modifier  
**Monk / Barbarian:** may have special AC from class features—use those instead.

## Trinket

At character creation you may take **one free trinket** from the [Trinkets table](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/creating-a-character#TrinketsTable) (roll d100 or pick with DM).
