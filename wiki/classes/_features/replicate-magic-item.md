# Replicate Magic Item

> [[artificer]] feature · Gained at level 2 · ⚠️ playtest

Replaces the old "Infuse Item" feature from TCoE. You learn **plans** to make magic items.

## Plans known and items active

Both scale with Artificer level (see the [[artificer]] level table). At L2: **4 plans known, 2 items active**.

## Creating an item

When you finish a [[long-rest]], craft any item from a known plan. The created item is a real magic item (functions identically) except its magic isn't permanent: it vanishes 1d4 days after you die.

- If you exceed your max active items, the oldest one vanishes when you create a new one.
- If a created item requires Attunement, you can attune to it the instant you create it.

[UA 2024 Artificer]

## Available plans by tier

The L2 plan list includes: **Alchemy Jug · Bag of Holding · Cap of Water Breathing · Goggles of Night · Rope of Climbing · Sending Stones · Shield +1 · Wand of Magic Detection · Wand of Secrets · Wand of the War Mage +1 · Weapon +1** — plus *any* Common magic item that isn't a Potion, Scroll, or cursed.

Higher-tier plan tables unlock at L6, L10, L14 (see source PDF for full lists).

## Upgrades at higher levels

The "Magic Item X" features upgrade Replicate Magic Item:

| Level | Feature | Upgrade |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 6 | **Magic Item Tinker** | Drain a created item as a [[bonus-action]] to create a spell slot (L1 if Common; L2 if Uncommon/Rare) — slot vanishes on long rest. Also: Expanded Replication (L6+ table). |
| 10 | **Magic Item Adept** | Attune to up to **4** magic items at once. Expanded Replication (L10+ table). |
| 14 | **Magic Item Savant** | Attune to up to **5** magic items at once. Expanded Replication (L14+ table). Max **3 Rare** replicated items. |
| 18 | **Magic Item Master** | Attune to up to **6** magic items at once. |
