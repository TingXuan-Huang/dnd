# Divine Order

> [[cleric]] feature · Gained at level 1

At level 1, choose one of two Divine Orders:

- **Protector.** Gain proficiency with martial weapons and heavy armor.
- **Thaumaturge.** Gain an extra cleric cantrip. Your damage-dealing cantrips become more potent (deal additional damage at higher levels).
