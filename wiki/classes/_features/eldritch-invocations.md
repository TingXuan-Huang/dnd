# Eldritch Invocations

> [[warlock]] feature · Gained at level 1

Choose **two** Eldritch Invocations at level 1. Invocations are persistent magical features — some grant new spells, others modify existing ones. (For example, the invocation *Agonizing Blast* boosts *Eldritch Blast*.) [BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Warlock]

The complete invocation list lives in PHB 2024.
