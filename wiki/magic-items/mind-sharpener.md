# Mind Sharpener

> **Ring, Uncommon** · Requires Attunement · ⚠️ playtest (UA 2024 Artificer)

The item has **4 charges**. When you fail a [[constitution]] [[saving-throw|save]] to maintain [[concentration]], you can take a Reaction and expend 1 charge to **succeed instead**. The item regains **1d4** expended charges daily at dawn. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Notes

In TCoE this was an Artificer **infusion** that applied to armor or robes. In UA 2024 it's been converted into a proper magic ring — Artificers can [[replicate-magic-item|replicate]] it.
