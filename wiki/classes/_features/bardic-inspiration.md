# Bardic Inspiration

> [[bard]] feature · Gained at level 1

As a [[bonus-action]], grant one ally within range an inspiration die. Within the next 1 hour, the ally can add the die to one of their failed [[d20-test]]s (after seeing the roll). The die starts at **d6** and increases at higher levels. [BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Bard]

## Uses / recharge

- Uses per [[long-rest]] = your [[charisma]] modifier (min 1).
