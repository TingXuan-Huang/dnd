## Scene 1 — Fallowton Village (Surface)

### Short prompt

```
Small medieval farming village inside a rough wooden palisade, rainy overcast day, mud roads, leaning fences, visible dark forest tree line to west and south, low hills to the north with a distant dark scar on the slope (partial sinkhole, not fully visible). East: narrow river with wooden plank bridge. Cinematic wide shot, moody, realistic fantasy, no modern elements, desaturated colors, film grain.
```

### Detailed prompt

```
Establishing wide shot of Fallowton, a poor walled farming village of about twenty-five households in a rainy frontier landscape.

COMPOSITION: View from slightly east of the village, looking west-northwest so the north hillside and a ominous dark bite in the distant slope are visible but not readable in detail. Foreground: muddy rutted lane with broken wooden fence posts leaning inward. Midground: circular wooden palisade enclosing clustered buildings. Background: low forested hills pressing from west and south; north rises into a small mountain or large hill with barley fields; on that north slope a barn-sized terraced mud collapse with a hint of pale stone and a faint cold blue glimmer deep inside (villagers cannot see the full pit, only a wound-like scar). Far east: flat rainy plain and a narrow river crossed by a simple wooden ford bridge.

VILLAGE INSIDE WALL (must be suggested, not labeled):
- A two-story inn with sagging thatch (The Bent Plow), warm lantern glow in one window
- A larger communal hall with steep roof (Elder's Hall)
- A small stone or timber shrine with a simple bell or cross (Sister Elra)
- A modest guest cottage with dark shutters (wizard's lodging)
- A waterwheel or mill shed with wheel half-stuck in mud, motionless
- A village well with wooden roof
- Scattered grain store, smith shed, kitchen smoke from chimneys
- A muddy green, chickens, laundry lines, barrels

ATMOSPHERE: Weeks of rain; everything slick; puddles mirror gray sky; no sunshine. Mood: quiet dread, not battle. Peasants in cloaks, not knights. No high fantasy spires.

STYLE: Realistic cinematic fantasy, 14th-century rural Europe feel mixed with original world, high detail, atmospheric perspective, muted earth tones, subtle cold blue accent only on distant hill scar.

AVOID: Bright colors, sunny sky, large castle, modern buildings, cartoon style, text, labels, maps, UI, dragons in sky, obvious magic fireworks.
```

## Scene 2 — Sinkhole / Mountain Opening (Exterior Descent)

### Short prompt

```
Massive barn-sized sinkhole terraced into a rainy hillside at the base of a dark mountain, spiral stone stairs carved along one inner wall, three ancient arched stone doorways set into the pit walls at depth, faint blue light at the bottom, broken wooden cart and snapped fence at rim, cinematic low angle from inside the pit looking up at a small coin of gray sky, wrong-scale oppressive mountain looming, realistic dark fantasy.
```

### Detailed prompt

```
Dramatic view from INSIDE a large open-air sinkhole on a rain-soaked hillside, low angle looking upward (hill-as-wrong-scale).

SCALE: The opening is roughly barn-sized (30–40 feet across), terraced slump of raw mud and broken roots on three sides; the fourth side shows **cut ancient stone stairs** integrated into the inner wall, descending in wide terraced steps (not a natural cave mouth — an engineered descent exposed by collapse).

RIM: Jagged muddy lip with dead grass tufts, hanging black roots, snapped wooden fence, an abandoned broken farm cart half-slid into the mud. Rain drips from the edge. At the top, a small distant circle of gray sky — claustrophobic, the mountain face above feels impossibly tall and close, oppressive vertical rock.

INNER WALLS: Not bare dirt only — patches of **pale gray dressed stone** emerging from mud, covered in carved rings, spirals, and kneeling figures with **smooth blank ovals instead of faces**. Tiny star-shaped drill holes in the stone catch dim light. Some reliefs show shadows carved flowing upward toward a central point.

THREE ARCHES: Deeper on the inner circumference, three heavy **stone arched doorways** (tomb-like, Dawnweave ancient), set into the retaining wall at different heights on the terrace. One arch may show a **fractured door** with a narrow gap (dark beyond). Stairs zigzag or spiral down toward them.

DEPTH: At the lowest visible point, a **faint cold blue glow** (machinery-like, not fire), suggesting something vast below — the "heart" of the ruin. No characters required; optional tiny silhouettes with torches at rim for scale.

ATMOSPHERE: Recent landslide after weeks of rain; fresh mud slides; smell-visual of wet stone and iron. Horror-adjacent mystery, not lava or hell.

STYLE: Realistic cinematic fantasy, high detail rock and mud texture, desaturated, dramatic rim light from above, subtle blue bounce light from depth.

AVOID: Bright blue lightning, crystal caves, ice, snow unless faint on far peaks, modern machinery, steampunk, readable text on walls, cartoon proportions, perfect symmetry museum ruin.
```

### Optional second angle — View from terrace path (villager approach)

```
View from the north field path toward the slump: terraced mud steps descending one flank of a circular pit; cut stone stairs visible on the inner right wall; opposite wall shows one pale arched doorway glimpsed through rain; village invisible below the hill; only forest and mountain; torches optional; same rainy palette and realistic dark fantasy style.
```

## Scene 3 — Underground Hall, Pyramid, Ring Tunnel & Three Doors

### Short prompt

```
Vast underground collapsed hall, half-buried pale gray stepped pyramid temple rising from the floor, dry stone ritual pool with handprints in dust before it, curved tunnel wrapping the structure, three ancient stone doors in the ring wall (one door shattered with narrow gap), star-hole carvings and faceless kneeling statues, faint blue light from cracks, hanging roots through broken ceiling, realistic dark fantasy archaeology horror.
```

### Detailed prompt

```
Interior of a buried Dawnweave ritual chamber — not a tidy dungeon corridor but a **collapsed underground hall** half filled with soil and roots.

SPACE: Cavern roughly 60–80 feet wide, ceiling partially collapsed; massive tree roots punch through like ribs. Air hazy with dust. Floor: cracked flagstones, mud pockets, fallen columns (broken cylindrical pillars) lying as cover-stones.

CENTERPIECE: A **half-buried stepped pyramid** or ziggurat-like structure of pale cold gray stone, geometric, ancient, not Egyptian gold — more alien-proportional. Upper half exposed; lower half swallowed by floor rubble. Face covered in **concentric ring inscriptions**, **spiral grooves**, **tiny star drill-holes**, and rows of kneeling figures whose heads are **smooth blank ovals (never carved faces)**. Shadows carved on the floor flow in lines toward the pyramid. On the exposed face: a grand **stairway descending into the pyramid heart** (dark opening; faint blue light from within).

FORECOURT: Directly before the pyramid, a **dry stone basin pool** (altar font), empty of water, filled with fine gray dust showing **overlapping palm and hand prints** (adult and child sizes).

RING TUNNEL: A curved passage **wraps around** the pyramid at floor level — arched, claustrophobic, ceiling low in places. Along the ring, **three heavy stone doors** with arched tops:
- LEFT branch door: **broken**, slab cracked, a **narrow crawl gap** (2 feet high) with darkness and optional tiny blue motes beyond
- MIDDLE and RIGHT doors: massive closed slabs, seams that look like they could grind shut; iron-free stone, purely ancient

LIGHTING: No torches in scene unless specified; primary light is **dim ambient** plus **cold blue leakage** from stone seams (like old magic wiring), especially near left gap and pyramid heart stairs. No warm fire except if user adds torch-holders.

MOOD: The ruin feels half-dead, half-dreaming — archaeological, sacred, wrong proportions (doorways almost human height but subtly off). Not cluttered with treasure chests.

STYLE: Realistic cinematic fantasy interior, wide angle or three-quarter view showing pyramid + pool + at least two doors, high texture, muted palette, atmospheric dust rays from ceiling breaks.

AVOID: Egyptian gold, Mayan bright paint, sci-fi neon, lava, underground lake, modern bricks, grid floor tiles, game-map aesthetic, text labels, cartoon, overly clean museum lighting.
```

### Optional insert — Single door close-up (broken gap)

```
Close-up of an ancient stone door in a curved underground tunnel, slab fractured with a narrow irregular gap at the bottom, cold blue pinprick lights visible through the crack like distant fireflies, carved ring patterns on the lintel, dust and root fibers, realistic dark fantasy, shallow depth of field.
```

### Optional insert — Dry pool detail

```
Top-down or low angle of an empty stone ritual pool in underground hall, dust surface with many overlapping handprints, pale pyramid steps visible in background, faint blue light reflecting on dust, somber realistic fantasy.
```

## Consistency checklist (all three scenes)

When generating a series, keep these fixed:

1. **Stone color:** pale gray, cold, not sandstone orange  
2. **Magic light:** faint cyan-blue in seams only — low intensity  
3. **Faces on carvings:** always blank smooth ovals  
4. **Weather (surface scenes):** rain, mud, gray sky  
5. **North geography:** village low; hill/mountain north; slump on slope  
6. **Three doors:** always arched stone, ancient, same style in Scene 2 (depth) and Scene 3 (hall ring)
