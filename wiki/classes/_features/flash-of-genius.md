# Flash of Genius

> [[artificer]] feature · Gained at level 7 · ⚠️ playtest

When you OR a creature you can see within 30 feet fails an [[ability-check]] or [[saving-throw]], you can use your **Reaction** to add a bonus equal to your [[intelligence]] modifier to the roll, potentially turning the failure into a success. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Uses

- Uses per [[long-rest]] = your [[intelligence]] modifier (minimum 1).

## Change from TCoE

In the 2020 Tasha's version, this only applied to saving throws. The UA 2024 version expands it to **both ability checks and saving throws** — significantly more flexible.
