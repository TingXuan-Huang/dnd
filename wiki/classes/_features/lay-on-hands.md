# Lay on Hands

> [[paladin]] feature · Gained at level 1

You have a pool of healing power that replenishes on each [[long-rest]]:

- **Pool size:** 5 × your paladin level.
- **Use:** As a Magic action, spend any number of points from the pool to heal a creature you touch by that many HP. (Per PHB 2024 the action also cures poison/disease at fixed point costs.)
