# Languages

Every character starts with **Common** plus at least 2 additional standard languages (chosen or rolled). Classes and species may grant more. [BR-2024 §01-creating-a-character#Choose Languages]

## Standard languages (d12)

| Roll | Language | Origin |
| --- | --- | --- |
| — | Common | Sigil |
| 1 | Common Sign Language | Sigil |
| 2 | Draconic | Dragons |
| 3–4 | Dwarvish | Dwarves |
| 5–6 | Elvish | Elves |
| 7 | Giant | Giants |
| 8 | Gnomish | Gnomes |
| 9 | Goblin | Goblinoids |
| 10–11 | Halfling | Halflings |
| 12 | Orc | Orcs |
