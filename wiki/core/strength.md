# Strength

> Measures: Physical might. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#The Six Abilities]

## Where Strength applies

- **[[ability-check|Strength checks]]:** lifting, pushing, breaking.
- **[[saving-throw|Strength saves]]:** resisting being moved or forced.
- **Melee weapon [[attack-roll|attacks]]:** Strength provides the attack and damage modifier (unless the weapon has the [[finesse]] property and you choose Dexterity instead).
- **Skill:** [[athletics]].
