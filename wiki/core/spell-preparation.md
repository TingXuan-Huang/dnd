# Spell Preparation

You can only cast spells you have **prepared** (or spells you cast via a magic item like a spell scroll). Class features determine when and how many spells you can swap. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Gaining and Preparing Spells]

## When you can change your prepared list

| Class | When you change | How many can change |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [[bard]] | Gain a level | 1 |
| [[cleric]] | Finish a [[long-rest]] | Any |
| [[druid]] | Finish a [[long-rest]] | Any |
| [[paladin]] | Finish a [[long-rest]] | 1 |
| [[ranger]] | Finish a [[long-rest]] | 1 |
| [[sorcerer]] | Gain a level | 1 |
| [[warlock]] | Gain a level | 1 |
| [[wizard]] | Finish a [[long-rest]] | Any |

[BR-2024 §06-spells#Gaining and Preparing Spells]

**Always-prepared spells** granted by class or subclass features do **not** count against your prepared list. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Gaining and Preparing Spells]
