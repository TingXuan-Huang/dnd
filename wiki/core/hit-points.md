# Hit Points (HP)

A creature's pool of damage absorption. Damage subtracts; healing restores up to the maximum. At 0 HP, you make death saves until stabilized, healed, or killed.

## HP at level 1

| Class | HP at L1 |
| --- | --- |
| [[barbarian]] | 12 + [[constitution|Con]] modifier |
| [[fighter]], [[paladin]], [[ranger]] | 10 + Con modifier |
| [[bard]], [[cleric]], [[druid]], [[monk]], [[rogue]], [[warlock]] | 8 + Con modifier |
| [[sorcerer]], [[wizard]] | 6 + Con modifier |

[BR-2024 §01-creating-a-character#Level 1 Hit Points]

## HP per level (after level 1)

Roll your class's hit die OR take the fixed average:

| Class | Roll | Fixed average |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [[barbarian]] | 1d12 + Con | 7 + Con |
| [[fighter]], [[paladin]], [[ranger]] | 1d10 + Con | 6 + Con |
| [[bard]], [[cleric]], [[druid]], [[monk]], [[rogue]], [[warlock]] | 1d8 + Con | 5 + Con |
| [[sorcerer]], [[wizard]] | 1d6 + Con | 4 + Con |
