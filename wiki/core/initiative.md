# Initiative

A [[d20-test]] made at the start of combat to determine turn order. [BR-2024 §01-creating-a-character#Key Numbers]

## Formula

`d20 + Dex modifier` (and add [[proficiency-bonus]] if you have Initiative proficiency — see [[alert]]).

Higher results act earlier. Ties: the higher Dexterity acts first; if still tied, the DM decides or the players roll off.

## Special rules

- The [[alert]] feat grants Initiative proficiency and lets you swap rolls with a willing ally.
- Some class features and items modify Initiative.
