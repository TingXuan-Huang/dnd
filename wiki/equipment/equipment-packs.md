# Equipment Packs

Pre-bundled gear sets purchasable at character creation. [BR-2024 §05-equipment#Equipment Packs]

## The six packs

| Pack | Cost | Typical contents |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Burglar's Pack** | 16 GP | Backpack, ball bearings, bell, candles, crowbar, oil, rations, rope, tinderbox, waterskin |
| **Dungeoneer's Pack** | 12 GP | Backpack, crowbar, oil, rations, rope, tinderbox, torch, waterskin |
| **Entertainer's Pack** | 40 GP | Backpack, bedroll, costumes, candles, rations, waterskin |
| **Explorer's Pack** | 10 GP | Backpack, bedroll, mess kit, oil, rations, rope, tinderbox, torch, waterskin |
| **Priest's Pack** | 33 GP | Backpack, blanket, candles, tinderbox, rations, waterskin, incense, vestments |
| **Scholar's Pack** | 40 GP | Backpack, book, ink, pen, parchment, lamp, oil, tinderbox |
