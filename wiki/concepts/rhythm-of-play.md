# Rhythm of Play

D&D play follows a repeating loop:

1. The DM describes the scene.
2. Players describe what their characters want to do.
3. The DM resolves the results — often via a [[d20-test]]. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Rhythm of Play]

## Three pillars

Activities tend to fall into three pillars: **social interaction**, **exploration**, and **combat**. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Rhythm of Play]
