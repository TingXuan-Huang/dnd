# Paladin

> Primary: [[strength]] and [[charisma]] · Hit die: d10 · Saves: Wisdom, Charisma

## Proficiencies

- **Weapons:** Simple and Martial
- **Armor:** Light, Medium, Heavy, Shields
- **Saves:** [[wisdom]], [[charisma]]
- **Skills:** choose 2 of [[athletics]], [[insight]], [[intimidation]], [[medicine]], [[persuasion]], [[religion]]

## Starting equipment

- **A.** Chain Mail, Shield, Longsword, 6 Javelins, Holy Symbol, Priest's Pack, 9 GP
- **B.** 150 GP

[BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Paladin]

## Level 1 features

- [[lay-on-hands]] — Healing pool = 5 × paladin level (refreshes on [[long-rest]]).
- [[spellcasting]] — Half-caster. Prepare (Wis mod + half paladin level, min 1) spells.
- [[divine-sense]] — Detect celestials, fiends, and undead within range.
- [[weapon-mastery]] — Two weapon masteries.

- [[oath-of-devotion]] (chosen at level 3)
