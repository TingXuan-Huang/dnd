# Alchemist

> [[artificer]] subclass · Available at level 3 · Source: UA 2024 Artificer (playtest)

The Alchemist brews experimental elixirs and excels at acid, fire, necrotic, and poison damage. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Features by level

### L3: Tool Proficiency

Gain proficiency with **Alchemist's Supplies** (or another Artisan's Tools if already proficient). Crafting potions takes **half** the normal time.

### L3: Alchemist Spells (always prepared)

| Artificer Level | Spells |
| --- | --- |
| 3 | Healing Word, Ray of Sickness |
| 5 | Flaming Sphere, Melf's Acid Arrow |
| 9 | Gaseous Form, Mass Healing Word |
| 13 | Death Ward, Vitriolic Sphere |
| 17 | Cloudkill, Raise Dead |

[UA 2024 Artificer]

### L3: Experimental Elixir

Brew **2 elixirs** on each [[long-rest]] using Alchemist's Supplies; effects rolled (or chosen at higher levels) from a d6 table. See [[experimental-elixir]] for full effects.

You can spend a spell slot during the day to brew an extra elixir (choose the effect). Elixir count per long rest scales: **3 at L5, 4 at L9, 5 at L15**.

### L5: Alchemical Savant

When you cast an Artificer spell using Alchemist's Supplies as your [[spellcasting|focus]], add your [[intelligence]] modifier to **one** of the spell's healing rolls OR one acid/fire/necrotic/poison damage roll (minimum +1).

### L9: Restorative Reagents

- Anyone who drinks an [[experimental-elixir]] you brew gains **Temporary Hit Points** equal to `2d6 + your Artificer level`.
- Cast **Lesser Restoration** without a spell slot, Int-mod times per [[long-rest]], using Alchemist's Supplies as the focus.

### L15: Chemical Mastery

- **Alchemical Eruption.** When you cast an Artificer spell dealing acid, fire, necrotic, or poison damage, also deal **2d8 Force damage** to one target (once per turn).
- **Chemical Resistance.** [[resistance]] to acid and poison damage.
- **Conjured Cauldron.** Cast *Tasha's Bubbling Cauldron* without spell slot or components, using Alchemist's Supplies as focus. Once per long rest.
