# Grappler

> Category: General · Prerequisites: Level 4+; Strength or Dexterity 13+

- **Ability Bonus:** Increase Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Grappler]
- **Punch and Grab:** When you hit with an [[unarmed-strike]], you can deal its damage and [[grapple]] the target with the same attack — once per turn. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Grappler]
- **Attack Advantage:** You have advantage on attack rolls against creatures you are grappling. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Grappler]
- **Fast Wrestler:** Moving a creature your size or smaller while grappling costs no extra movement. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Grappler]
