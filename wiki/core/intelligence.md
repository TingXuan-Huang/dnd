# Intelligence

> Measures: Reasoning, memory. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#The Six Abilities]

## Where Intelligence applies

- **[[ability-check|Intelligence checks]]:** recalling lore, analyzing problems, reasoning through evidence.
- **[[saving-throw|Intelligence saves]]:** disbelieving illusions, resisting attacks on the mind.
- **Spellcasting ability** for Wizard and (in 5.5e) Artificer.
- **Skills:** [[arcana]], [[history]], [[investigation]], [[nature]], [[religion]].
