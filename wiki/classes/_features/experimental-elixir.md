# Experimental Elixir

> [[alchemist]] ([[artificer]] subclass) feature · Gained at level 3 · ⚠️ playtest

Whenever you finish a [[long-rest]] while holding Alchemist's Supplies, magically produce **2 elixirs**. Each appears in a vial; roll on the table below for its effect (the vial vanishes when the elixir is drunk or poured out, or when you finish another long rest). [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Drinking an elixir

As a [[bonus-action]], a creature can drink an elixir or administer it to another creature within 5 feet.

## d6 effect table

| d6 | Effect |
|----|----|
| 1 | **Healing.** Regain `2d8 + Int mod` HP. |
| 2 | **Swiftness.** Speed +10 ft for 1 hour. |
| 3 | **Resilience.** +1 AC for 10 minutes. |
| 4 | **Boldness.** Roll 1d4 and add to every attack roll and save for 1 minute. |
| 5 | **Flight.** Fly speed 10 ft for 10 minutes. |
| 6 | **Choose** one of the other rows. |

[UA 2024 Artificer]

## Extra elixirs

As a Magic action with Alchemist's Supplies in hand, expend a spell slot to create one additional elixir, **choosing** the effect rather than rolling.

## Per-rest scaling

Elixirs brewed on each [[long-rest]]:

- L3: 2
- L5: 3
- L9: 4
- L15: 5
