Actions

***Multiattack.*** The wyvern makes one Bite attack and one Sting attack.

***Bite.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +7, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) Piercing damage.

***Sting.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +7, reach 10 ft. *Hit:* 11 (2d6 + 4) Piercing damage plus 24 (7d6) Poison damage, and the target has the [Poisoned](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#PoisonedCondition) condition until the start of the wyvern’s next turn.
