# Weapon Mastery

A new class feature in the 2024 revision. Allows a martial character to apply a special **mastery property** to weapons they have mastered. [BR-2024 §03-character-classes]

## Who gets it at level 1

| Class | Masteries |
| --- | --- |
| [[barbarian]] | 2 melee |
| [[fighter]] | 3 |
| [[paladin]] | 2 |
| [[ranger]] | 2 |
| [[rogue]] | 2 |

Other martial classes gain more masteries as they level. Casters typically do not gain Weapon Mastery.

## How it works

- Each weapon has one mastery property (e.g., Cleave, Nick, Topple, Vex).
- You apply the property when you hit with a weapon you have mastered.
- You can swap which weapons you have mastered on a [[long-rest]].

Specific mastery properties are defined in chapter 05 (equipment).

## Changes from 5e

**Brand-new mechanic** — Weapon Mastery did not exist in 5e (2014). It's one of the headline additions in 5.5e.
