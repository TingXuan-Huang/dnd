# Spell Slot

A "container" for casting leveled spells. Each spell slot has a level (1–9). Casting a level-1 spell expends one slot of level 1 or higher; casting a higher-level spell requires a slot of equal or higher level. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Spell Level and Slots]

## How they recover

- Most casters refresh **all** expended slots on a [[long-rest]].
- [[warlock]]'s [[pact-magic]] slots refresh on a **short rest**.
- [[wizard]]'s [[arcane-recovery]] can recover slots on a short rest, once per day.

## Cantrips do not use slots

[[cantrip]]s cost no slot. Rituals can be cast without a slot (see [[ritual-casting]]).

## One-slot-per-turn limit

You can expend at most one spell slot per turn — your Magic action plus at most one slot-expending spell cast via a [[bonus-action]] of any kind. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Casting Spells]
