# Charisma

> Measures: Presence, force of personality. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#The Six Abilities]

## Where Charisma applies

- **[[ability-check|Charisma checks]]:** influencing others, performing, projecting confidence.
- **[[saving-throw|Charisma saves]]:** asserting your identity (resisting effects that would transform, possess, or banish you).
- **Spellcasting ability** for Bard, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock.
- **Skills:** [[deception]], [[intimidation]], [[performance]], [[persuasion]].
