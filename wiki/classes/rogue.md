# Rogue

> Primary: [[dexterity]] · Hit die: d8 · Saves: Dexterity, Intelligence

## Proficiencies

- **Weapons:** Simple, plus Martial weapons that have [[finesse]] or [[light]]
- **Armor:** Light
- **Tools:** Thieves' Tools
- **Saves:** [[dexterity]], [[intelligence]]
- **Skills:** choose 4 of [[acrobatics]], [[athletics]], [[deception]], [[insight]], [[intimidation]], [[investigation]], [[perception]], [[persuasion]], [[sleight-of-hand]], [[stealth]]

## Starting equipment

- **A.** Leather Armor, 2 Daggers, Shortsword, Shortbow, 20 Arrows, Quiver, Thieves' Tools, Burglar's Pack, 8 GP
- **B.** 100 GP

[BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Rogue]

## Level 1 features

- [[expertise]] — Double [[proficiency-bonus]] on two of your skill proficiencies.
- [[sneak-attack]] — 1d6 extra damage, once per turn, when you have [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] OR an ally is within 5 ft of the target.
- [[thieves-cant]] — Secret language understood only by other Rogues / those taught it.
- [[weapon-mastery]] — Two weapon masteries.

## Subclass

- [[thief]] (chosen at level 3)
