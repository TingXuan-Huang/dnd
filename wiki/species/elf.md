# Elf

> Size: Medium (5–6 ft) · Speed: 30 ft · Lifespan: ~750 years · Creature type: Humanoid

[BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Elf]

## Traits

- **[[darkvision]] 60 ft.** [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Elf]
- **Fey Ancestry.** [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on saves to avoid the [[charmed]] condition. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Elf]
- **Keen Senses.** Proficiency in one of: [[insight]], [[perception]], or [[survival]]. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Elf]
- **Trance.** Take a [[long-rest]] in 4 hours of meditation rather than 8 hours of sleep. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Elf]
- **Elven Lineage.** Choose one Lineage at character creation (see below). [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Elven Lineages]
