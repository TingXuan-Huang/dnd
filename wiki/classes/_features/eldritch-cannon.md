# Eldritch Cannon

> [[artillerist]] ([[artificer]] subclass) feature · Gained at level 3 · ⚠️ playtest

Using Woodcarver's Tools or Smith's Tools, take a Magic action to create a Small or Tiny Eldritch Cannon in an unoccupied space within 5 feet. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Creation rules

- One cannon at a time.
- After creation, you can't make another until you finish a [[long-rest]] OR expend a spell slot.
- Cannon disappears at 0 HP or after **1 hour**. Dismiss early as a Magic action.

## Cannon stat block

> **Small or Tiny Object** · **AC** 18 · **HP** 5 × your Artificer level · **Immunities** Poison, Psychic

*Casting [[mending|Mending]] on it restores 2d6 HP.*

## Activating the cannon

Within 60 ft of it, as a [[bonus-action]], order it to use **one** option below. You can move it up to 15 ft before or after that option.

| Option | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Flamethrower** | 15-ft Cone. Dex save vs. your spell DC. 2d8 Fire damage (half on success). Flammable objects ignite. |
| **Force Ballista** | Ranged spell attack from cannon, range 120 ft. On hit: 2d8 Force damage, push creature up to 5 ft from cannon. |
| **Protector** | Burst of positive energy. Cannon and creatures within 10 ft gain Temporary HP = 1d8 + your Intelligence modifier. |

[UA 2024 Artificer]

## Upgrades

- **L9: Explosive Cannon.** Cannon damage +1d8. **Detonate** (Reaction): when cannon drops to 0 HP within 60 ft, it explodes — 3d10 Force damage in 20-ft radius (Dex save halves).
- **L15: Fortified Position.** Have **two** cannons. Activate both with one [[bonus-action]]. You and allies have Half [[cover]] within 10 ft of either cannon.
