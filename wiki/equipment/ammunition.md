# Ammunition

> Weapon property

The weapon makes ranged [[attack-roll|attacks]] using ammunition (arrows, bolts, sling bullets, etc.). When you draw ammunition, you have one piece in hand for the attack. After combat, you can recover up to half of the expended ammunition with a quick search. [BR-2024 §05-equipment#Weapon Properties]
