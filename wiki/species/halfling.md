# Halfling

> Size: Small (2–3 ft) · Speed: 30 ft · Lifespan: ~150 years · Creature type: Humanoid

[BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Halfling]

## Traits

- **Brave.** [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on saves to avoid the [[frightened]] condition. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Halfling]
- **Halfling Nimbleness.** You can move through the space of any creature larger than you. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Halfling]
- **Luck.** When you roll a natural 1 on the d20 of any [[d20-test]], you can reroll the die and must use the new roll. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Halfling]
- **Naturally Stealthy.** You can take the [[hide-action]] while obscured by a creature one size larger than you. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Halfling]
