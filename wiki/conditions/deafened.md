# Deafened

While you have the Deafened condition:

- **Can't Hear.** You can't hear and automatically fail any [[ability-check]] that requires hearing.

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#DeafenedCondition]

## Common sources

- Loud noises (thunder bursts, *Thunderwave*-like effects)
- Spells: *Blindness/Deafness*

## Notes

Spells with Verbal components can still be cast — Deafened only blocks hearing, not speech. (Spells require *audible* incantation, but the caster is not blocked by being unable to hear themselves.)
