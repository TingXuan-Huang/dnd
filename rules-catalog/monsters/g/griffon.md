***Multiattack.*** The griffon makes two Rend attacks.

***Rend.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +6, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 8 (1d8 + 4) Piercing damage. If the target is a Medium or smaller creature, it has the [Grappled](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#GrappledCondition) condition (escape DC 14) from both of the griffon’s front claws.
