# Goliath

> Size: Medium (7–8 ft) · Speed: 35 ft · Creature type: Humanoid

[BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Goliath]

## Traits

- **Giant Ancestry.** Choose one boon at character creation; uses = [[proficiency-bonus]] per [[long-rest]]: [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Goliath]
  - *Cloud's Jaunt* — short teleport (reaction)
  - *Fire's Burn* — extra fire damage on a melee hit
  - *Frost's Chill* — slow target on hit
  - *Hill's Tumble* — knock target [[prone]] on melee hit
  - *Stone's Endurance* — reduce incoming damage as reaction
  - *Storm's Thunder* — damage attacker as reaction
- **Large Form** *(Level 5+).* Become Large size for 10 minutes. Once per [[long-rest]]. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Goliath]
- **Powerful Build.** [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on saves to end the [[grappled]] condition; count as one size larger for carrying capacity. [BR-2024 §02-character-origins#Goliath]
