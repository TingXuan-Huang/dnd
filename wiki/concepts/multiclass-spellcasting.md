# Multiclass Spellcasting

If you have the Spellcasting feature from more than one class, you combine caster levels to determine spell slots — but you **prepare spells per class** separately. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Multiclass Spellcasting]

## Caster-level contribution by class

| Class | Contribution |
| --- | --- |
| [[bard]], [[cleric]], [[druid]], [[sorcerer]], [[wizard]] | Full level |
| [[paladin]], [[ranger]] | Half level (round up) |
| Eldritch Knight ([[fighter]] subclass), Arcane Trickster ([[rogue]] subclass) | One-third level (round down) |

Use the combined level on the multiclass spell-slot table in chapter 08 (`08-supplements.md`). [BR-2024 §06-spells#Multiclass Spellcasting]

## Pact Magic interaction

[[pact-magic]] slots from [[warlock]] are **separate** from the multiclass spell-slot table. Pact Magic slots can cast spells you have prepared from other classes; conversely, regular spell slots can cast Pact-known spells. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Multiclass Spellcasting]
