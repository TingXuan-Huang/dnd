## Official rules (online)

This guide summarizes the free **2024 Basic Rules** on D&D Beyond. When in doubt, the site wins:

- [Creating a Character](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/creating-a-character)
- [Playing the Game](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/playing-the-game) (abilities, skills, dice)
- [Character Classes](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/character-classes)
- [Character Origins](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/character-origins) (backgrounds & species)
- [Equipment](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/equipment)
- [Tracking Sheets](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/tracking-sheets)

**Tip:** Building on [D&D Beyond](https://www.dndbeyond.com/characters) auto-calculates most numbers. Bring a printed or digital sheet to the table either way.

## Our campaign tone

- Starting level: **1** (unless the DM says otherwise)
- Setting: **Fallowton** — a small farming village where magic is costly, mistrusted, and old legends are waking up
- Your character can be a local, a traveler, a hired guard, or someone drawn by rumors of the sinkhole

Ask the DM about anything not covered here (home rules, banned options, evil alignments).
