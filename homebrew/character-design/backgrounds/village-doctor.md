# Background: Village Doctor

*Homebrew · Fallowton campaign · Formatted like D&D 2024 Basic Rules backgrounds*

Use this background if your character is the person Fallowton sends for when a child does not wake up, when a fieldhand goes cold after harvest, or when the wizard's apprentice starts bleeding from the nose for no reason anyone wants to name out loud.

---

## Village Doctor

You set bones, deliver babies, and know which root tea is for sleep and which is for the kind of grief that stops a heart in a week. You are not a priest and you are not a wizard. You are the one with the kit, and the steady hands, and the patience to sit through a long night without lying about what is coming.

You have seen Weave Strain before anyone gave it that name. The grey eyes. The cold breath. The way the animals back up from a wizard's apprentice without anyone telling them to. You did not call it black craft, because calling things by the wrong name makes them harder to treat. You wrote it in your book under *symptoms*.

Fallowton trusts you more than it trusts the chapel and more than it trusts the crown. That trust is a kind of debt too. You try not to think about it that way.

**Ability Scores:** Wisdom, Intelligence, Constitution  

**Feat:** [Skilled](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/feats#Skilled) (you cover for an entire village's lack of specialists — medicine, herb-lore, kind words, hard truths)  

**Skill Proficiencies:** [Medicine](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills), [Insight](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/playing-the-game#Skills)  

**Tool Proficiency:** [Herbalism Kit](https://www.dndbeyond.com/equipment/477-herbalism-kit) (your salves and teas — also useful for component prep if anyone ever taught you that part)

## Optional personality prompts

- Name a patient you lost. Not the most famous loss — the one you still re-read your notes about, looking for what you missed.
- What do you charge people who cannot pay? Trade, time, a promise? Have any of them paid you back yet?
- You have seen Weave Strain symptoms in someone Fallowton does not know is a caster. Who, and why have you not told the village?

## Weave Strain (if you gain magic later)

You do not start as a spellcaster. Your hands work on the *outside* of the channel — the body that pays the price, not the channel itself. If you take a caster class later, normal rules apply. If you do not, your role is to keep the casters at the table alive.

- You do **not** gain Luck debt from **observing** Weave Strain. Sitting beside a caster after they cast, examining the symptoms, taking notes for your book — none of it adds to your sheet. The village knows this and brings the worst cases to you.
- During a **short rest**, you may attempt a **Wisdom (Medicine) DC 14** check on one ally who failed a **burnout** Constitution save this session. On success, that ally **rerolls** the failed burnout save once. They take the second result, success or failure. **Once per session, once per ally.**
- You may also use the existing roleplay rules under Weave Strain's "what strain feels like" section: **DC 10 Medicine** to recognize strain, **DC 12 + kit + short rest** to grant an ally advantage on their next strain Con save or reduce strain by 1 (DM's call, once per long rest). See the [Weave Strain Magic doc's recovery section](../../../docs/campaign/weave-strain-magic.md#recovery) for the canonical write-up.
- If you eventually cast leveled magic yourself, all normal strain rules apply. You will be very good at noticing what is happening to you. You will not be any safer.

See [Weave Strain Magic — Village Doctor](../../../docs/campaign/weave-strain-magic.md#custom-fallowton-backgrounds).
