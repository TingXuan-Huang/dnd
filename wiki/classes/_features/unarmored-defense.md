# Unarmored Defense

Both [[barbarian]] and [[monk]] have an "Unarmored Defense" feature, but the formula differs.

## Barbarian variant

While not wearing armor, your AC = **10 + Dex modifier + Con modifier**. You may use a shield and still benefit. [BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Barbarian]

## Monk variant

While not wearing armor and not using a shield, your AC = **10 + Dex modifier + Wis modifier**. [BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Monk]
