# Returning Weapon

> **Weapon (any Simple or Martial with the [[thrown]] property), Uncommon** · Requires Attunement · ⚠️ playtest (UA 2024 Artificer)

This magic weapon grants a **+1 bonus** to attack and damage rolls made with it. The weapon **returns to your hand** immediately after it is used to make a [[thrown]] attack. [UA 2024 Artificer]
