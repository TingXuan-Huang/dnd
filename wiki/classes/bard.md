# Bard

> Primary: [[charisma]] · Hit die: d8 · Saves: Dexterity, Charisma

## Proficiencies

- **Weapons:** Simple
- **Armor:** Light
- **Tools:** 3 Musical Instruments (your choice)
- **Saves:** [[dexterity]], [[charisma]]
- **Skills:** choose any 3 from the 18

## Starting equipment

- **A.** Leather Armor, 2 Daggers, Musical Instrument of your choice, Entertainer's Pack, 19 GP
- **B.** 90 GP

[BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Bard]

## Level 1 features

- [[bardic-inspiration]] — d6 die; an ally can add it to a failed d20 within 1 hour. Uses = Cha modifier (min 1) per [[long-rest]].
- [[spellcasting]] — 2 cantrips, 4 prepared spells, 2× level-1 slots. Charisma-based. Musical instrument as spell focus.

Recommended starter cantrips: *Dancing Lights*, *Vicious Mockery*.
Recommended starter spells: *Charm Person*, *Color Spray*, *Dissonant Whispers*, *Healing Word*.

- [[college-of-lore]] (chosen at level 3)
