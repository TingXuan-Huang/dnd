# Artificer

> ⚠️ **Playtest material** — UA 2024, dated 2024-12-17. Not officially part of the game; mechanics may change.

> **Primary:** [[intelligence]] · **Hit die:** d8 · **Saves:** [[constitution]], [[intelligence]]

Masters of invention. Artificers use ingenuity and magic to unlock extraordinary capabilities in objects. [UA 2024 Artificer, 2024-12-17]

## Proficiencies

- **Weapons:** Simple
- **Armor:** Light, Medium, Shields
- **Tools:** Thieves' Tools, Tinker's Tools, and one type of Artisan's Tools of your choice
- **Saves:** [[constitution]], [[intelligence]]
- **Skills:** choose 2 of [[arcana]], [[history]], [[investigation]], [[medicine]], [[nature]], [[perception]], [[sleight-of-hand]]

## Starting equipment

- **A.** Studded Leather Armor, Dagger, Thieves' Tools, Tinker's Tools, Dungeoneer's Pack, 16 GP
- **B.** 150 GP

[UA 2024 Artificer]

## Spellcasting at a glance

- **Half-caster** — max 5th-level spell slots at level 17+.
- **Intelligence-based.** Spell save DC = 8 + PB + Int. Spell attack = PB + Int.
- **Cantrips:** Start with 2; learn 3rd at L10, 4th at L14. Can replace one cantrip on each [[long-rest]].
- **Prepared spells:** start at 2, scaling with level (see table).
- **Spellcasting focus:** Thieves' Tools, Tinker's Tools, or your Artisan's Tools. Must have one in hand when casting (Artificer spells have an M component).

See [[artificer-spells]] for the spell list. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Class feature highlights

- **L3 Right Tool for the Job** — extends [[magical-tinkering]] to create Artisan's Tools.
- **L6 Magic Item Tinker** — drain a replicated item to a spell slot (L1 for Common item, L2 for Uncommon/Rare); also expands replication options.
- **L10 Magic Item Adept** — attune to 4 magic items at once; expands replication.
- **L14 Magic Item Savant** — attune to 5; max 3 Rare replicated items.
- **L18 Magic Item Master** — attune to 6.

Each "Magic Item X" tier expands which plans you can choose for [[replicate-magic-item]].

## Subclasses

- [[alchemist]] — Brews [[experimental-elixir|elixirs]]; bonuses to acid/fire/necrotic/poison damage and healing
- [[armorer]] — Wears Arcane Armor; choice of Dreadnought / Guardian / Infiltrator
- [[artillerist]] — Summons an [[eldritch-cannon]]; ranged magical artillery
- [[battle-smith]] — Commands a [[steel-defender]] construct ally

## Changes from TCoE Artificer

[UA 2024 Artificer]

- **Magical Tinkering** crafts low-cost items quickly.
- **Spellcasting** allows swapping one cantrip per long rest.
- **Infuse Item → Replicate Magic Item** — replicate any qualifying magic item by rarity and type (no infusion-list limit).
- **Magic Item Tinker** replaces Tool Expertise; converts items to spell slots.
- **Flash of Genius** now reacts to both ability checks AND saves.
- **Spell-Storing Item** now stores up to level 3 spells (was level 2).
- **Soul of Artifice** no longer requires a Reaction; restores you to 20 HP (was 1).
- Old TCoE [[infusions]] are gone — replaced by Replicate Magic Item plus the magic items in this UA.
