# Feats

**Source:** D&D Beyond Basic Rules (2024)  
**Date:** 2026-05-20 | **Version:** 1.0

---

Feats are special capabilities beyond your class. Your **background** grants an Origin feat. Classes grant **Ability Score Improvement** or other feats at certain levels.

Each feat can be taken only once unless marked **Repeatable**.

## Origin Feats

### Alert

- **Initiative Proficiency:** Add proficiency to Initiative rolls.
- **Initiative Swap:** After rolling Initiative, swap with one willing ally in same combat (neither Incapacitated).

### Magic Initiate

- **Two Cantrips:** From Cleric, Druid, or Wizard list. Choose spellcasting ability (Int/Wis/Cha).
- **Level 1 Spell:** Always prepared; cast once without slot per long rest (or use slots).
- **Spell Change:** On level-up, replace one spell from chosen list.
- **Repeatable:** Yes — different list each time.

### Savage Attacker

Once per turn when you hit with a weapon, roll damage dice twice and use either result.

### Skilled

Proficiency in any three skills or tools of your choice. **Repeatable.**

## General Feats

### Ability Score Improvement

**Prerequisite:** Level 4+

+2 one ability score, or +1 to two scores (max 20). **Repeatable.**

### Grappler

**Prerequisite:** Level 4+; Str or Dex 13+

- +1 Str or Dex (max 20).
- **Punch and Grab:** Unarmed Strike can deal damage and Grapple same attack (once/turn).
- **Attack Advantage:** Advantage vs. creatures you Grapple.
- **Fast Wrestler:** No extra movement to move Grappled creature your size or smaller.

## Fighting Style Feats

**Prerequisite:** Fighting Style feature (Fighter, Paladin, Ranger, etc.)

### Archery

+2 to attack rolls with Ranged weapons.

### Defense

+1 AC while wearing Light, Medium, or Heavy armor.

### Great Weapon Fighting

With two-handed melee weapons: treat damage 1 or 2 as 3 on each die.

### Two-Weapon Fighting

Add ability modifier to damage of Light weapon extra attack if not already added.

## Epic Boon Feats

**Prerequisite:** Level 19+ (class Epic Boon feature)

All include **+1 ability score (max 30)** unless noted.

| Feat | Key Benefit |
| --- | --- |
| Combat Prowess | Miss → hit once/turn |
| Dimensional Travel | After Attack/Magic action, teleport 30 ft |
| Fate | Add/subtract 2d4 to any d20 within 60 ft |
| Irresistible Offense | B/P/S ignore resistance; crit adds extra damage |
| Night Spirit | Hide in dim light/dark; resistance except psychic/radiant |
| Spell Recall | On slot 1–4 cast, 1d4 chance slot not spent |
| Truesight | 60 ft Truesight |
