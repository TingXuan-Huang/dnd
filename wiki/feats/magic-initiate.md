# Magic Initiate

> Category: Origin · Prerequisites: None · **Repeatable** (different spell list each time)

- **Two Cantrips:** Learn two cantrips from the Cleric, Druid, or Wizard spell list. Choose Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma as the spellcasting ability for them. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Magic Initiate]
- **Level 1 Spell:** Choose one 1st-level spell from the same list. It is always prepared. You can cast it once without a [[spell-slot]] per [[long-rest]], or expend a slot if you have one of the right level. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Magic Initiate]
- **Spell Change:** Each time you gain a level, you may replace the chosen 1st-level spell with another from the same list. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Magic Initiate]
- **Repeatable:** Yes — must choose a different spell list each time. [BR-2024 §04-feats#Magic Initiate]
