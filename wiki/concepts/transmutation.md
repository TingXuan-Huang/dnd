# Transmutation

> School of magic · Typical effects: Transformation

Transmutation spells change the properties of a creature, object, or environment — speed, shape, substance. Basic Rules examples: *Jump*, *Longstrider*, *Feather Fall*, *Expeditious Retreat*. [BR-2024 §06-spells#Schools of Magic]
