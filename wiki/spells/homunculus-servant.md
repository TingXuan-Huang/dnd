# Homunculus Servant

⚠️ Playtest. Source: UA 2024 Artificer.

> **Level 2 Conjuration** ([[artificer]]) · **Casting Time:** 1 hour or [[ritual-casting|Ritual]] · **Range:** 10 ft · **Components:** V, S, M (a gem or crystal worth 100+ GP, **consumed**) · **Duration:** Instantaneous

You summon a special homunculus in an unoccupied space within range. It uses the **Homunculus Servant** stat block below. If you already have a homunculus from this spell, the homunculus is replaced by the new one. [UA 2024 Artificer]

You determine the homunculus's appearance.

## Homunculus Servant stat block

> **Tiny Construct, Neutral** · **AC** 13 · **HP** 5 + 5 × spell level (hit dice = d4 × spell level) · **Speed** 20 ft, fly 30 ft

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 4 (−3) | 15 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 10 (+0) | 10 (+0) | 7 (−2) |

**Immunities:** Poison; Exhaustion, Poisoned
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft, Passive Perception 10
**Languages:** Telepathy 1 mile (works only with you)
**CR:** None (XP 0; PB equals your Proficiency Bonus)

### Traits

- **Evasion.** On a Dex save for half damage: succeed → no damage; fail → half damage. Not usable while [[incapacitated]].
- **Magic Bond.** Add the spell's level to any ability check or save the homunculus makes.

### Actions

- **Force Strike.** *Melee or Ranged Attack:* bonus = your spell attack mod, reach 5 ft or range 30 ft. Hit: `1d6 + spell level` Force damage.

### Reactions

- **Channel Magic.** **Trigger:** You cast a touch-range spell while the homunculus is within 120 ft of you. **Response:** The homunculus delivers the spell through its touch.

## In combat

Acts on your initiative count, immediately after your turn. Obeys your commands (no action needed). With no command, it uses the [[dodge-action|Dodge]] action and movement to avoid danger. [UA 2024 Artificer]

## Upcast

Use the spell level in the HP and damage formulas (5 + 5 × level; 1d6 + level Force damage).
