# Attack Roll

A [[d20-test]] to hit a target. The attack hits if `d20 + modifiers ≥` target's [[armor-class]]. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Attack Rolls]

## Ability used by attack type

| Attack type | Ability |
| --- | --- |
| Melee weapon | [[strength]] (or [[dexterity]] for [[finesse]] weapons) |
| Ranged weapon | [[dexterity]] |
| Spell attack | Per spellcasting feature |

[BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Attack Rolls]

## Natural 20 / Natural 1

- **Natural 20** on the d20 (regardless of modifiers): [[critical-hit]].
- **Natural 1** on the d20 (regardless of modifiers): automatic miss. [BR-2024 §07-playing-the-game#Attack Rolls]
