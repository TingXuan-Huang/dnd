# Artificer Infusions

⚠️ Playtest. Source: UA 2024 Artificer.

In Tasha's Cauldron of Everything (2020), [[artificer|Artificers]] had a class feature called **Infuse Item** with a list of "infusions" they could apply to mundane items. The UA 2024 Artificer **removes infusions** entirely and replaces them with two mechanisms:

## 1. Replicate Magic Item

The new [[replicate-magic-item]] feature lets the Artificer make actual magic items from a list of "plans" — not infusions on mundane gear. This subsumes most of the old infusions:

| TCoE infusion | UA 2024 replacement |
| --- | --- |
| Enhanced Arcane Focus | Replicate **Wand of the War Mage +1** |
| Enhanced Defense | Replicate **+1 Armor** or **+1 Shield** |
| Enhanced Weapon | Replicate **+1 Weapon** |
| Homunculus Servant | Now a real spell — [[homunculus-servant]] |

[UA 2024 Artificer]

## 2. Magic items (formerly infusions)

Several TCoE infusions were converted into actual magic items in this UA. They're not Artificer-exclusive — anyone can attune to them, and Artificers can craft them via [[replicate-magic-item]]:

- [[boots-of-the-winding-path]]
- [[helm-of-awareness]]
- [[mind-sharpener]] (now a Ring, was an infusion on armor/robes)
- [[radiant-weapon]]
- [[repeating-shot]]
- [[repulsion-shield]]
- [[returning-weapon]]
- [[spell-refueling-ring]]
