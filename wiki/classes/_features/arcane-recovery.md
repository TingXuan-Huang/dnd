# Arcane Recovery

> [[wizard]] feature · Gained at level 1

Once per day on a short rest, recover expended spell slots whose combined level is at most `½ wizard level` (rounded up). No recovered slot can be 6th level or higher. [BR-2024 §03-character-classes#Wizard]

At level 1, this typically recovers a single 1st-level slot.
