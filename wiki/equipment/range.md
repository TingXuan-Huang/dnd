# Range

> Weapon property

The weapon's Range entry shows two numbers in feet: normal range and long range. [[attack-roll|Attacks]] at distances beyond normal range but within long range have [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]]. You cannot attack a target beyond long range. [BR-2024 §05-equipment#Weapon Properties]
