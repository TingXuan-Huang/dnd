## NPC tracker

### Bessa Thorn

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Role | Bent Plow barmaid; rumor gate; introduces travelers and village suspicion |
| Stat block | **Commoner**; DEX 12, CHA 14, Persuasion +4 |
| Gate | Buy a drink for 2 sp, or Persuasion DC 13 / Deception DC 14 / Intimidation DC 15 |
| Rumors | Mostly false or useless by design; use them for folk color, not dungeon solutions |

**Bent Plow Hangover:** If a PC drinks the strong house ale before the delve, for the first 4 hours of the next adventure day they have disadvantage on **Wisdom (Perception)** checks and **-2 passive Perception**. A CON save DC 10 as an action clears it early; retry once per short rest. Do **not** apply disadvantage to Wisdom saving throws.

### Grask the Split-Ear (mini-boss)

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|

**Custom stat (level 1 boss):**

| | |
|--|--|
| **Grask the Split-Ear** | Medium Humanoid (Goblinoid), typically Neutral Evil |
| AC | 16 (stolen chain scraps) |
| HP | 22 |
| Speed | 30 ft. |
| STR 12 DEX 14 CON 12 INT 10 WIS 10 CHA 8 |
| Saves | DEX +4 |
| Skills | Stealth +6, Intimidation +1 |
| Senses | Darkvision 60 ft., Passive Perception 10 |
| Languages | Common, Goblin |
| CR | ~1 (with minions) |

**Actions**

- **Hooked Blade.** *Melee.* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 1d8+2 slashing. On hit, Grask can **disengage** as part of the attack (once per turn).
- **Nail Spit (Recharge 5–6).** *Ranged 20 ft.* +4 to hit. *Hit:* 1d4+2 piercing; target must succeed on **CON DC 11** or be **poisoned** until end of Grask’s next turn.

**Bonus**

- **Screech.** Each ally goblin within 30 ft. can move up to half speed toward a target Grask sees.

**Balance note:** Grask is fair as a solo Medium fight for 3 level-1 PCs. If a goblin escaped to warn him, add only **1 Goblin Minion** or **1 Goblin Warrior**, not two.

### Empty Wind — creature pressure

| Encounter | Creatures | Use |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| Dust storm | 2 × **Dust Mephit** | Main fight if you want a chaotic elemental room |
| Breathless Warden | 1 × **Animated Armor** adjusted to HP 26 | Main fight if players touch the map, fail the phrase, or clock is high |
| Full Wind run | Mephits flee before armor activates | Do not run mephits and armor in the same round |

**Dust Mephit reference:** AC 12, HP 17, CR 1/2, 100 XP. Two Dust Mephits are already a full Medium encounter for 3 level-1 PCs.

**Breathless Warden reference:** Based on **Animated Armor**. Use AC 18, HP **26** as a deliberate 3-PC safety adjustment from the official HP 33, speed 25 ft., Slam per Basic Rules. The puzzle phrase can power it down without killing it.

**Breathless traits:** Immune to Charmed; cannot speak; advantage on saves vs. thunder. It is **Map-Bound** and stays dormant until the map is touched, the puzzle fails, or the clock reaches a dangerous stage.

**Silence Aura:** Do not use the spell-fail version in Session 1. If you want the flavor, make it narrative only: voices drop to whispers, and a leveled spell causes the wind to flare or advances attention, but the spell still works.
