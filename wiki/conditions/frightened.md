# Frightened

While you have the Frightened condition:

- **Ability Checks and Attacks Affected.** [[advantage-disadvantage|Disadvantage]] on [[ability-check|ability checks]] and [[attack-roll|attack rolls]] **while the source of fear is within line of sight**.
- **Can't Approach.** You can't willingly move closer to the source of fear.

[BR-2024 §rules-glossary#FrightenedCondition]

## Notes

- The Disadv triggers only when the source is visible — break line of sight (e.g., behind a wall) and you can still attack normally, you just can't approach the source.
- You can still cast spells, attack other targets, etc.

## Common sources

- Spells: *Cause Fear*, *Fear*, *Phantasmal Killer*
- Monster abilities: dragon Frightful Presence, Demilich, banshees, undead

## Defenses

- [[halfling]] **Brave** grants [[advantage-disadvantage|Advantage]] on saves to avoid being Frightened.
- **Mindless Rage** (Berserker Barbarian L6 in PHB 2024 — [[path-of-the-berserker|stub]] in this wiki) grants immunity while raging.
