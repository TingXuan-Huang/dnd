***Death Burst.*** The mephit explodes when it dies. *Dexterity Saving Throw:* DC 11, each creature in a 5-foot [Emanation](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#EmanationAreaofEffect) originating from the mephit. *Failure:* 7 (2d6) Fire damage. *Success:* Half damage.

***Claw.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +3, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 3 (1d4 + 1) Slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) Fire damage.

***Fire Breath (Recharge 6).*** *Dexterity Saving Throw:* DC 11, each creature in a 15-foot [Cone](https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/free-rules/rules-glossary#ConeAreaofEffect). *Failure:* 7 (2d6) Fire damage. *Success:* Half damage.
